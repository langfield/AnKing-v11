What <u>laboratory finding</u> has a <i>high <b>sensitivity</b></i>
for the diagnosis of <b>congestive heart failure</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::Elevated brain natriuretic peptide (BNP) levels}}
</div>

### Extra
<i>BNP levels > <b>400</b> pg/ML are suggestive of CHF, whereas
levels < <b>100</b> pg/mL have a high negative predictive value
for CHF; compare with clinical signs of CHF (crackles, JVP, edema,
S3) which are <b>specific</b> for CHF</i>
