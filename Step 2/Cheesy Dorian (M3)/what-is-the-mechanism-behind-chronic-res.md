### Text
What is the mechanism behind <u>chronic respiratory alkalosis</u>
in the setting of <b>pregnancy</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::Progesterone indirectly increases minute ventilation by
  increasing the sensitivity of the respiratory center to carbon
  dioxide}}
</div>

### Extra
This physiological response results in a slightly higher
pO<sub>2</sub> and a slightly lower pCO<sub>2</sub> to facilitate
gaseous exchange between maternal blood and the fetoplacental unit.
An additional effect of <b style="">hyperventilation</b> is
<u>chronic respiratory alkalosis</u>, which is compensated by renal
acidification.
