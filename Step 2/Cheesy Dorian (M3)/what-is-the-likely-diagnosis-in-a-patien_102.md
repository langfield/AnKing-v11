What is the likely <i>diagnosis</i> in a patient with a history of
<u>recurrent pulmonary infections</u> that presents with <b>facial
swelling</b>, <b>bilateral lower-extremity edema</b>,
<b>hepatomegaly</b>, and <b>palpable kidneys</b>? Urinalysis
reveals 4+ proteinuria.

### Extra

are consistent with nephrotic syndrome (think deposition
everywhere) - these are <u>secondary</u> to the underlying
inflammatory condition.</i>
