What is the most important <i>initial step</i> in management of a
patient with <b>hyperosmolar hyperglycemic state</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::Fluid replacement with <u><b>normal saline</b></u>}}
</div>

### Extra
<i>initially, replace with <b>NS</b> regardless of sodium levels
due to massive <b>diuresis</b> from high glucose levels; may switch
to 0.45% saline after a few hours if <u>corrected</u>
Na<sup>+</sup> levels are high (recall 2 for every 100 above 100);
<b>IV insulin</b> <b>+/- K<sup>+</sup></b> may also be given
later.</i>
