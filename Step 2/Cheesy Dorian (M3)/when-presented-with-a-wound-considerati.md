### Text
<div>
  When presented with a wound, consideration for tetanus
  vaccination begins with determining whether <b>{{c1::three}}
  lifetime doses</b> have been given or not.
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  - <b>If <3 lifetime doses</b>, then the timing of the wound
  relative to the last booster/vaccination doesn't matter (clean →
  Tdap; dirty → Tdap + TIG)
</div>
<div>
  - <b>If 3+ lifetime doses</b>, then TIG (tetanus IVIg) will never
  be needed
</div>
