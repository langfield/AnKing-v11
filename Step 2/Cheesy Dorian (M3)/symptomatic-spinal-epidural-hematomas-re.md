### Text
Symptomatic <u>spinal</u> epidural hematomas require urgent
surgical decompression of the spinal canal and evacuation of the
hematoma by {{c1::laminectomy}}

### Extra
<u>Laminectomy</u>: removal of the dorsal part of the vertebra
(lamina) to widen the spinal canal, thereby relieving pressure on
the spinal cord and spinal nerves
