### Text
What procedure is both <i>diagnostic</i> and <i>therapeutic</i> for
<b>septic</b> <b>arthritis</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::Arthro<font color="#FC0280">centesis</font> (to drain
  synovial fluid)}}
</div>

### Extra
<i>- <b>Arthrocentesis</b>:a diagnostic and/or therapeutic
procedure in which <b>synovial fluid</b> from a joint is aspirated
using a sterile needle to determine the <b>etiology</b> of joint
effusions, as well as to relieve pain or pressure from a joint</i>
<div>
  <i>- Should be performed <u>prior</u> to <b>empiric antibiotic
  therapy</b> to minimize false negative results</i>
</div>
