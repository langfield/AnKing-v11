### Extra
A warm compress may soften the pus-filled nodule and accelerate
drainage of the inflamed gland. The warm compress should be applied
gently to the affected eyelid, 3–5 times a day, and 10–15 minutes
at a time. Avoid chamomile compresses due to the high risk of
allergic reactions.
