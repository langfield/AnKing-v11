### Text
What is the <i>next step</i> in management for a patient that
develops <b>fatigue</b>, <b>insomnia</b>, and <b>myalgias</b> after
<u>abruptly</u> discontinuing paroxetine due to undesirable side
effects?
<div>
  {{c1::Restart paroxetine and <u>taper</u> gradually over several
  weeks}}
</div>

### Extra
<i>this patient is experiencing <b>antidepressant discontinuation
syndrome</b>, which can result in variable psychological and/or
physical symptoms; more common with antidepressants with a shorter
half-life (e.g. paroxetine, venlafaxine)</i>
