What is the <i>next step</i> in management for a mother with
<b>post-partum hemorrhage</b> following a <u>forceps-assisted
vaginal delivery</u>? The patient is afebrile and the uterus is
normal-sized and firm.
<div>
  {{c1::Genital tract inspection}}
</div>

### Extra
<i><u>genital tract injury</u> is a common cause of PPH after
<b>operative</b> vaginal deliveries; other causes of PPH typically
result in an enlarged, soft uterus (e.g. atony, retained placental
tissue) and/or fever (e.g. endometritis)</i>
