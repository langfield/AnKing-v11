### Text
<b>Mechanical ventilation</b> in patients with <u>ARDS</u> should
use {{c1::low::low/high}} <b>tidal volumes</b> and
{{c1::high::low/high}} <b>PEEP</b>

### Extra
<div>
  <i>- Low tidal volume ventilation (LTVV) <font color="#FF0000"
  style="">decreases the likelihood of overdistending
  alveoli</font> and <b>improves mortality</b> - imagine a low tide
  slowly washing into the alveoli, nice and easy.</i>
  <div>
    <i>- Positive end expiratory pressure (PEEP) improves
    <b>oxygenation</b> by preventing alveolar collapse at the end
    of expiration. "PEEP the alveoli open"</i>
  </div>
