What is the <i>next step</i> in management for a patient with
<u>acute renal failure</u> and<u>oliguria (< 250 mL/12
hours)</u> with evidence of <b>significant urine retention (e.g.
suprapubic discomfort)</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::Bladder catheterization}}
</div>

  <i>- often seen post-operatively</i>
