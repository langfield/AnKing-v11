Treatment for patients with <u>prolonged QT intervals</u> includes
{{c1::β-blocker}} therapy and <b>pacemaker</b> <b>placement</b>

### Extra

can prolong QT interval by <b>blocking</b> repolarizing K channels
(open curtain)</i>
