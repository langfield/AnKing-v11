### Text
What is the <i>recommended pharmacotherapy</i> for a patient with a
history of alcohol abuse that requests a medication to <b>reduce
cravings</b>? He is currently drinking 6-7 beers a day. There is no
evidence of hepatitis, cirrhosis, and patient does <u>not</u> take
opioids.
<div>
  {{c1::Naltrexone}}
</div>
