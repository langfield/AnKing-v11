### Extra
<i>Part of the chest is effectively uncoupled from the chest wall
and no longer follows the normal rib cage movement. Upon
inspiration, as the <b>intrathoracic pressure drops</b>, the
unstable segment will be pulled inwards by the negative pressure,
whereas it would usually expand together with the rest of the
chest.</i>
