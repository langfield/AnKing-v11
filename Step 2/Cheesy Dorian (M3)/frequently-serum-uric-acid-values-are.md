    finding in patients with SIADH</u>. This decrease in uric acid
    is due to an <b>increased urinary uric acid clearance</b>,
    which is hypothesized to be a result of excess water retention
    + volume expansion. Hypouricemia typically resolves after
    correction of hyponatremia in patients with SIADH.
