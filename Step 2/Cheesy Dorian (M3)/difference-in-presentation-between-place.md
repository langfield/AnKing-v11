### Text
Difference in presentation between <b>placenta previa</b> and
<b>vasa previa</b>?
<div>
  Placenta previa = {{c1::heavy, persistent}} bleeding
</div>
<div>
  Vasa previa = {{c1::minimal, transient}} bleeding
</div>

### Extra
- Placenta previa reflects <u>maternal blood loss</u> and has signs
of <u>maternal hemorrhagic shock</u> (e.g. hypotension,
tachycardia)
<div>
  - Vasa previa is reflects <u>fetal blood loss</u>
</div>
