The diagnosis of <b>upper-airway cough syndrome</b> (postnasal
drip) is confirmed by <i>elimination</i> of <u>nasal discharge</u>
and <u>cough</u> with the use of {{c1::first-generation
H<sub>1</sub> receptor antagonists (e.g. chlorpheniramine)}}
