### Text
Persistent <u>fever</u>, <u>leukocytosis</u>, and <u>LUQ</u> pain
with a recent <u>infection</u> (endocarditis, cholecystitis) +/-
tender splenomegaly is suggestive of {{c1::splenic
abscess::diagnosis}}
