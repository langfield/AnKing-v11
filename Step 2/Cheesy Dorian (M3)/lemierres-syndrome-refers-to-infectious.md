### Text
<b>Lemierre's syndrome</b> refers to <i>infectious
thrombophlebitis</i> of the <u>internal jugular vein</u> and most
often develops as a complication of a bacterial {{c1::sore throat}}
infection in young, otherwise healthy adults

### Extra
<div>
  <i>- Infection → invade IJV → thrombus → septic microemboli</i>
</div>
<div>
  <i>- <b>F. necrophorum</b> is the most frequent bacterial cause
  of LS</i>
</div>
<div>
  <i>- Usually begins as <u>tonsilitis</u> and can present with
  significant <u>neck swelling or tenderness</u> in the week
  following infection</i>
