### Text
All patients with <u>acute COPD exacerbation</u> should receive
<b>inhaled bronchodilators (albuterol/ipratropium nebulizer)</b>
and <b>systemic {{c1::corticosteroids}}</b>

### Extra
<i>Corticosteroids have been shown to improve lung function and
promote a shorter hospital stay; supplemental <b>O<sub>2</sub></b>,
antibiotics and ventilatory support should be administered when
indicated</i>
