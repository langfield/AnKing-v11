### Text
What is the likely <i>diagnosis</i> in an intrapartum patient that
presents after >18 hours of membrane rupture and prolonged labor
with <b>fever</b> and <b>tachycardia</b>? Fetal heart tracing
demonstrates <u>fetal tachycardia</u>.
<div>
  {{c1::Chorioamnionitis (intra-amniotic infection)}}
</div>

### Extra
<div>
