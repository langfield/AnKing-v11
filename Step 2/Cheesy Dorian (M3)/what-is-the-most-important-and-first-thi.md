### Text
What is the most important and first thing to ask a pt that
complains of <b>dizziness?</b>
<div>
  {{c1::What do you mean? (e.g. determine between presyncope and
  vertigo)}}
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  <i>- Do you feel lightheaded or like you're going to pass out? If
  yes, it's <b>presyncope</b></i>
  <div>
    <i>- Do you have SOB or CP? If yes, likely
    <b>presyncope</b></i>
  </div>
  <div>
    <i>- Does it feel like the room is spinning? If yes, likely
    <b>vertigo</b></i>
  </div>
  <div>
    <i>- Do you feel unsteady on your feet? If yes, likely
    <b>vertigo</b></i>
  </div>
