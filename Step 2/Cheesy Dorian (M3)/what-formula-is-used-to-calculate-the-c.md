### Text
What formula is used to calculate the "<b>corrected
Na<sup>+</sup></b>" in a patient with <u>severe hyperglycemia</u>?
<div>
  {{c1::Na<sup>+</sup> + <b><font color="#FC0280">2</font></b>
  mEq/L for every <b><font color="#FC0280">100</font></b> mg/dL
  glucose is <u>over 100</u> mg/dL}}
</div>

### Extra
<i>e.g. if glucose is 600 mg/dL and Na<sup>+</sup> is 130 mEq/L,
corrected sodium is 140 mEq/L (5 x 2 = 10)</i>
