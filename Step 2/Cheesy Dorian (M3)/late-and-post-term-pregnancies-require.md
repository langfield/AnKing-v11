Late- and post-term pregnancies require <u>antenatal fetal
surveillance</u> beginning at <b>41 weeks</b> with a(n)
{{c1::biophysical profile}} to screen for <b>fetal</b>
<b>hypoxia</b>

<u>oligohydramnios</u>, late decelerations)</i>
