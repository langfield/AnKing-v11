concentration</u> in a pregnant patient?

### Extra
The <b>benefits</b> of treating with phenytoin <u>outweigh the
risks</u>, as seizures in mom are dangerous for the baby;
additionally, the majority of infants born to women who take
phenytoin during pregnancy <font color="#FC0280">will not develop
fetal hydantoin syndrome</font>
