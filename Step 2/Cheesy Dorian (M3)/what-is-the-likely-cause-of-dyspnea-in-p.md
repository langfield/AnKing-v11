What is the likely <i>cause</i> of <u>dyspnea</u> in patients with
<b>ankylosing spondylitis</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::Limited chest and spinal mobility}}
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  <i>Causes a <u>restrictive</u> pattern on PFTs with <b>decreased
  VC and TLC</b> but a <font color="#FC0280">normal or increased
  FRC and RV</font> due to <u>fixation</u> of rib cage in an
  inspiratory position - note that this is different than typical
  restrictive lung disease where FRC and RV are <b>lower</b> due to
  inability to fill the lungs; here, we're stuck at the end of a
  breath with still a lot of volume</i>
</div>
