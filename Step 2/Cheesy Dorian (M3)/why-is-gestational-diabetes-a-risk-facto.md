Why is <u>gestational diabetes</u> a risk factor for neonatal
<b>respiratory distress syndrome (NRDS)</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::Increased <u>insulin</u> <b>prevents</b> <b>surfactant</b>
  <b>synthesis</b> and lung maturation by interfering with
  <u>cortisol</u>}}
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  <i>Maternal hyperglycemia causes fetal hyperglycemia which in
  turn causes hyperinsulinemia which antagonizes cortisol and
  blocks maturation of sphingomyelin, a vital component of
  surfactant</i>
