<b>Indomethacin</b> is <u>contraindicated</u> at which gestational
age?
<div>
  {{c1::> 32 weeks gestation}}
</div>

### Extra
Tocolysis is not indicated after 34 weeks gestation as risks of the
therapies exceed those of preterm delivery. Indomethacin, a common
tocolytic, is contraindicated after 32 weeks gestation due to
<b>potential closure of the ductus arteriosus</b>.
