### Text
The <b>Cushing reaction</b> is a <u>triad</u> of
{{c1::hypertension}}, {{c2::bradycardia}}, and {{c3::respiratory}}
depression due to <u>increased</u> <b>{{c4::intracranial}}
pressure</b>

### Extra
<i>e.g. due to brain herniation during <b>hemorrhagic
stroke</b></i>
<div>
  <i><b>MOA</b>:</i> <i>increased ICP → ↓ cerebral perfusion
  pressure → activation of SNS → HTN to maintain CPP → activation
  of carotid baroreceptors → bradycardia</i>
</div>
