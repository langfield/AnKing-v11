<b>Fetal weight estimation</b> should <u>not</u> be performed more
frequently than <u>every 2 weeks</u>
<div>
  {{c1::T::T/F}}
</div>

### Extra
This is because fetal weight is indirectly estimated from abdominal
circumference, head circumference, fetal femoral length, and
biparietal diameter, and these parameters have an error of ± 2
weeks during the 2<sup>nd</sup> trimester and ± 3 weeks during the
3<sup>rd</sup> trimester
