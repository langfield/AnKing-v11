<div>

### Extra
Hyperthyroidism causes a decrease in systemic vascular resistance, but blood pressure (primarily systolic) rises due to positive inotropic and chronotropic effects. In contrast, hypothyroidism causes hypertension due to an increase in systemic vascular resistance
