### Text
The <i>second-line</i> <i>surgical treatment</i> for patients with
<b>epidural hematoma</b> is urgent {{c1::burr hole evacuation}}

### Extra
Second-line treatment because it does not always allow complete
evacuation of the hematoma and/or ligation of the bleeding
meningeal vessel
<div>
  <div>
    In centers with limited neurosurgical expertise, burr hole
    evacuation is an acceptable alternative, because it can be
    performed by general surgeons, but generally it is not the
    preferred surgical approach.
  </div>
