### Text
Before <b>extubation</b>, patients <u>intubated for medical
reasons</u> should undergo a(n) {{c1::spontaneous breathing trial}}

### Extra
<div>
  During an SBT, patients remain intubated but ventilatory support
  is turned off, allowing patients to breathe on their own for a
  short period of time (e.g. 1-2 hours)
</div>
<div>
  SBT is not needed in patients who were intubated for an
  <b>elective surgical procedure</b>
</div>
