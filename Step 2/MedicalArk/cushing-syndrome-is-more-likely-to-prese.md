### Extra
Excess cortisol binds to mineralocorticoid receptors, causing
increased retention of water and sodium, as well as increased
excretion of potassium and H<sup>+</sup>
<div>
  These mechanisms lead to hypernatremia and hypertension as well
  as hypokalemia and metabolic alkalosis
</div>
