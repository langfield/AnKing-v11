### Text
Administration of high-dose {{c1::oral conjugated estrogen}} is the
treatment of choice in <u>hemodynamically {{c2::stable}} women</u>
with acute <b>abnormal uterine bleeding</b>

### Extra
High levels of estrogen trigger rapid growth of the endometrium and
thereby stop sudden, heavy bleeding from the uterine surface
<div>
  Surgical treatment is needed if the patient does not respond to
  medical treatment or is hemodynamically unstable, bleeding is
  severe, medical management is contraindicated, or the underlying
  medical condition requires surgical repair
</div>
