### Text
Can <b>image-guided biopsy</b> be used for <i>diagnosis</i> of a
patient with a <b>malignant adnexal mass</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::No}}
</div>

### Extra
Image-guided biopsy may be contraindicated due to the malignant
features of her presentation (age, family history, ascites,
dyspnea, mass with firm nodularity)
<div>
  In the case of epithelian ovarian carcinoma, biopsy could lead to
  rupture of the mass and result in spreading of the cancerous
  cells throughout the abdomen; ideally, the mass is removed intact
  during exploratory laparotomy
</div>
