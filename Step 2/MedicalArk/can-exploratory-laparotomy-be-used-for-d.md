### Text
Can <b>exploratory laparotomy</b> be used for <i>diagnosis</i> of a
patient with a <b>malignant adnexal mass</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::Yes}}
</div>

### Extra
Exploratory laparotomy with cancer resection and inspection of the
entire abdominal cavity for metastases (i.e. surgical staging) is
required when there is a high clinical suspicion of ECG,
particularly with an acute presentation
<div>
  During surgery, the ovaries, uterus, omentum, and any visually
  apparent cancerous lesion will be removed and pelvic and
  paraaortic lymph nodes will be dissected
</div>
<div>
  Chemotherapy with platinum-based agents is initiated after
  surgery
</div>
