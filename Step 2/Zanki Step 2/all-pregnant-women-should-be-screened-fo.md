### Text
<i>All</i> pregnant women should be screened for <u>gestational
diabetes mellitus</u> at <b>{{c1::24}} - {{c1::28}} weeks
gestation</b>

### Extra
<i>placental hormones in the 2nd and 3rd trimester create
<u>increased maternal insulin resistance</u>; patients with risk
factors (e.g. obesity, previous GDM) should be screened earlier and
re-screened at 24-28 weeks if negative</i>
