### Extra
<div>
  - Low dose Aspirin inhibits the <b>OAT</b> secretion channels
  that carry uric acid from the blood into the renal proximal
  tubule cell; <u>thus at standard low dosages Aspirin is NOT used
  for pain control in gout</u>
</div>
<div>
  - Diuretics decrease GFR (less uric acid filtered through the
  glomerulus)
