<div>

### Extra
- Because bacteria are constantly remodeling their cell wall,
inhibition of only cell wall growth enzymes leave cell wall
degradation enzymes unaffected
<div>
  <div>
    - thus as the cell grows, hydrolytic enzymes continue to
    degrade the wall, leading to <u>structural weakness</u> and
    resultant <b>osmotic lysis</b>
  </div>
  <div>
    - additionally, the buildup of peptidoglycan precursors
    activates bacterial cell wall autolytic enzymes
