### Text
Use of <b>Linezolid</b> is contraindicated in patients taking drugs
that <i>increase</i> {{c1::<b>seroto</b>}}-<b>nergic</b>
transmission

### Extra
<div>
  - This is because linezolid is a weak MAO inhibitor, thus
  increasing levels of monoamines (aka <u>Serotonin</u>)
</div>
<div>
  - This can cause <b>Serotonin Syndrome</b> (<b>A</b>gitation,
  <b>A</b>utononomic stimulation, increased neuromuscular
  <b>A</b>ctivity)
</div>- Ex SSRIs (increasing serotonin availability), Triptans
(serotonin receptor agonists)
