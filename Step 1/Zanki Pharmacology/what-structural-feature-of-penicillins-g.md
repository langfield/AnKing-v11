What structural feature of <b>Penicillins</b> give them their
activity?
<div>
  {{c1::B-lactam ring}}
</div>

### Extra
- This is because the B-lactam core of the molecule irreversibly
binds (acetylates) a serine residue of the Transpeptidase active
site
<div>
  <div>
    - Acetylation of this site makes transpeptidases inactive,
    preventing cross-linking of the newly formed peptidoglycan
    layer
  </div>
</div>
