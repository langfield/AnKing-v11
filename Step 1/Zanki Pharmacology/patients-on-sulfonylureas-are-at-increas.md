### Text
<div>
  Patients on <b>sulfonylureas</b> are at <u>increased</u> risk of
  <b>hypoglycemia</b> if they have <b>{{c1::renal}} failure</b>
</div>

### Extra
<i>The way sulfonylureas are removed from the body is entirely by
the kidney (NO metabolizing or inactivation by other enzymes in the
liver), therefore, if there is renal failure sulfonylureas will
stay in the blood stream and thus have a longer half-life (leading
to more insulin release and increasing the likelihood of
hypoglycemia)</i>
