    <b>Rasburicase</b> differs from <b>Pegloticase</b> as it is not
    {{c1::PEG}}ylated
  </div>
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  - PEGylation prolongs half-life and limits immune reaction to
  drug; thus Rasburicase has a rapid-on/off action which makes it
  perfect for <u>Tumor Lysis Syndrome</u> (and not for kidney
  stones)
</div>
<div>
  - Both are administered IV
</div>
