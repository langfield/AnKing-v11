### Text
<u>Cinacalcet</u> is used to treat <b>hypercalcemia</b> secondary
to <b>{{c1::hyperparathyroidism}}</b>

### Extra
<div>
  Especially useful in patients with kidney disease (<i>Secondary
  Hyperparathyroidism</i>)
</div>
<div>
  <i>Ca<sup>2+</sup>-sensing receptors are located in the
  parathyroid gland <u>and</u> on the kidney; increased sensitivity
  causes decreased release of PTH</i>
