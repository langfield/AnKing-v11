### Text
Patients who are being infused with <b>norepinephrine</b> and
develop <u>rigid induration</u> and <u>pallor</u> around the IV
site can be treated with a local injection of {{c1::phentolamine}}

### Extra
This is caused by norepinephrine diffusing into the surrounding
tissue, causing intense a1 mediated vasoconstriction and local
tissue necrosis; This must be given with 12 hours of norepinephrine
<div>
  lidocaine will NOT be as effective here due to the local tissue
  necrosis (due to the local acidosis)
</div>
