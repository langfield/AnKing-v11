### Extra
<i>potassium wasting due to increased Na<sup>+</sup> delivery to
the collecting duct</i>
<div>
  <i>Inhibiting apical Carbonic Anhydrase results in an increased
  H+ remaining in the lumen, which inhibits the activity of the
  <b>NaH Antiporter,</b> therefore leading to natriuresis with
  resulting volume contraction, which increases Aldosterone
  production resulting in a stimulated Na+/K+-ATPase to restore
  volume (resulting in more Na+ pumped into the blood and K+ being
  taken out of it)</i>
</div>
