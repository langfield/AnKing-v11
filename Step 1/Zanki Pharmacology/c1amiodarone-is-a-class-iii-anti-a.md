### Text
{{c1::Amiodarone}} is a class III anti-arrhythmic that can produce
<b>hyper or hypothyroidism</b>

### Extra
<div>
  - Amiodarone is 40% iodine per wt
</div>
<div>
  - Hyperthyroidism will occur in patients with pre-existing
  thyroid disease who will then receive excess iodine to the
  thyroid
</div>
<div>
  - Hypothyroidism occurs due to a direct toxic effect of the drug
</div>
