To gauge the degree of {{c1::paralysis}} induced by neuromuscular
junction blocking agents, <b>train of four</b> stimulation can be
used during <u>anesthesia</u>

muscular response is recorded. Goal is usually 1/4 or 2/4 causing a
contraction
