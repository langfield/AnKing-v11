### Text
<u>Vancomycin Resistant Bacteria</u> typically spread resistance to
other bacterium via {{c1::<b>Transposition</b>}}

### Extra
Typically, the <u>resistance gene</u> (ex. VanA) and some flanking
chromosomal DNA is incorporated into a <u>plasmid</u> (ex. Tn1546)
and transferred into a <u>new bacterium</u> (ex. VRE --> Staph
Aureus)
