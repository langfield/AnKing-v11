### Text
<b>Thyroid Peroxidase</b> is involved in the first part of T4
synthesis, specifically by catalyzing the addition of
{{c2::<u>iodine</u>}} to tyrosine residues on
{{c1::<u>thyroglobulin</u>}}

### Extra
<div>
  This process is called <b>organification</b> and results in the
  formation of <b><u>MIT/DIT</u></b>.
</div>MIT: mono-iodotyrosine = 1 iodine added to tyrosine
<div>
  DIT: di-iodotyrosine = 2 iodine added to tyrosine
</div>
