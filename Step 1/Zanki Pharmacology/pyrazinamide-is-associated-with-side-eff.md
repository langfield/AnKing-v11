<b>Pyrazinamide</b> is associated with side effects of
<u>hepatotoxicity</u> and
<u>hyper</u>{{c1::<u>uric</u>}}<u>emia</u>

### Extra
<div>
  - Organic ions (ex Pyrazinoate - metabolite of Pyrazinamide)
  counter-ion for uric acid at the URAT1 antiporter on the apical
  surface of the collecting duct
</div>
<div>
  - thus the more Pyrazinamide secreted into the urine, more uric
  acid reabsorbed into the blood (same mechanism for Niacin
  ingestion and Lactate)
