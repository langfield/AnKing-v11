Patients should not consume <u>Milk</u>, <u>Antacids</u>, or
<u>Iron-containing preparations</u> with <b>Tetracyclines</b> as
they prevent the drugs {{c1::absorption}}

### Extra
- These liquids / drugs all have <b>divalent cations</b> (ex
Calcium)<b>,</b> which complex tetracyclines and prevent absorption
from the gut
