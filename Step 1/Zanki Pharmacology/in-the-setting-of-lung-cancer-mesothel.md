### Text
<div>
  In the setting of lung cancer / mesothelioma, <b>Bleomycin</b>
  can be used to prevent <u>recurrent</u> {{c1::pleural effusions}}
</div>

### Extra
<i>This is called <b>Pleurodesis</b>, where an agent
(<u>bleomycin</u>, slurry of <u>talc</u>) is introduced to a
pleural space (site of recurrent effusions) to obliterate the
pleural space (thus preventing any more effusions from occurring
there)</i>
