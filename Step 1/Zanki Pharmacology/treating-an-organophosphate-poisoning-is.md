### Text
Treating an organophosphate poisoning is two fold;
{{c1::<b>Atropine</b>}} which inhibits all subtypes of
{{c1::<b>muscarinic receptors</b>}}, and
{{c2::<b>Pralidoxime</b>}}, which if given early - regenerates
{{c2::<b>AChE</b>}}
