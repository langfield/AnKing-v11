### Text
<b>Bendazoles</b> are <u>antihelminthic</u> agents that work by
inhibiting {{c1::microtubules}}

### Extra
- <b>Mebendazole</b> is used for Pinworm, Giant Roundworm,
Threadworm, Hookworms, Trichinosis, Whipworm, Toxocara canis;
<b>Albendazole</b> is used for Echinococcus, Neurocysticercosis
<div>
  - can cause a peripheral neuropathy (due to microtubule
  destabilization)
