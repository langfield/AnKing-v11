### Text
<div>
  <b>{{c3::Methotrexate}}</b> is a(n) <b>{{c2::folic acid}}</b>
  analogue that blocks <u>pyrimidine synthesis</u> by competitively
  inhibiting <b>{{c1::dihydrofolate reductase}}</b>
</div>

<div>
  This prevents the production of <b>THF,</b> thus indirectly
  prevents the production of <b><u>Thymidine</u></b> (<i>no met-THF
  to act as cofactor for dUMP-->dTMP</i>)
</div>
