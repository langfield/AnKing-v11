### Text
Patients taking <b>Ethambutol</b> can manifest with
{{c1::red-green}} color blindness

### Extra
This is due to ethambutol damaging the <b>retinal ganglion
cells</b> via excitotoxic pathways
<div>
  <div>
    - This manifests early as blue-yellow color blindness early
    (which typical color blindness screens don't pick up), then
    <u>develops into a red-green color blindness</u>
  </div>
  <div>
    - Eventually, patients can develop a central scotoma
  </div>
