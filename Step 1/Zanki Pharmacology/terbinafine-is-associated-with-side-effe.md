<b>Terbinafine</b> is associated with side effects of GI upset,
headaches, hepatotoxicity, and {{c1::taste::type of sensation}}
disturbance

### Extra
- can cause complete loss of taste (ageusia), decreased taste
(hypogeusia), or distorted taste (dysgeusia) - often involving
metallic taste and dry mouth
<div>
  - these usually are reversible with discontinuation of treatment
