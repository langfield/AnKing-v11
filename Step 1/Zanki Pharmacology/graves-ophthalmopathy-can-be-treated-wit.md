### Extra
<span style=
"-webkit-tap-highlight-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0);"><i>Glucocorticoids
inhibit fibroblast activity resulting in decreased GAG deposition
behind eyes as well as decreased inflammation; in Graves, TSI binds
to TSH receptors on fibroblasts behind eye as well as pretibial
region, resulting in deposition of GAG in those areas.</i></span>
