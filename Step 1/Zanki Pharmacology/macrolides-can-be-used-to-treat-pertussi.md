### Text
<b>Macrolides</b> can be used to treat <u>Pertussis</u> as long as
it is still in the {{c1::catarrhal}} phase

### Extra
- Once it has reached the paroxysmal phase, Pertussis Toxin is
produced and has made it into the bloodstream
