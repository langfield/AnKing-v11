### Text
Patients taking <b>HIV</b> <b>Protease inhibitors</b> for awhile
can resemble patients on chronic <u>Glucocorticoid therapy</u> due
to their ability to cause {{c1::Lipodystrophy}}

### Extra
- Fat accumulates in abdomen, and a "buffalo hump" of fat can form
on upper back
<div>
  - Cessation of Protease Inhibitors does not reverse
  lipodystrophy, and is treated with <b>Tesamorelin</b> (a GHRH
  analog)
</div>
