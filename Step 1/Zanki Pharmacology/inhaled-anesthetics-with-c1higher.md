### Extra
<div>
  <i>e.g. halothane; large amounts must be absorbed before the
  blood saturated</i>
</div>

<div>
  this reflects <b>peripheral tissue uptake</b>; large amount of
  anesthetic is taken up from arterial blood, thus in order to
  saturate the blood you need to further absorb more anesthetic to
  replace that which is absorbed by peripheral tissues; because
  blood saturation takes longer --> brain saturation is delayed
