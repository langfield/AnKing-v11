### Text
<div>
  <b>Class IV antiarrhythmics</b> decrease pacemaker activity by
  <u>decreasing</u> the <b>slope</b> of <b>phase {{c1::0}}</b> of
  the <i>nodal</i> action potential
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  <i>Class IV antiarrhythmics of importance (verapamil and
  diltiazem) are L type selective, and thus decrease the slope of
  phase 0 (the Ca2+ dependent upstroke of nodal cells)</i>
</div>
