### Text
<div>
  <b>{{c2::Aprepitant}}</b> <i>antagonizes</i> <b>{{c1::NK1}}
  receptors</b> in the <b>area postrema</b>, which is used to treat
  chemotherapy-induced vomiting
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  <i>Lacks effects on serotonin, dopamine, and muscarinic receptors
  (less side effects); often combined with a 5-HT<sub>3</sub>
  antagonist</i>
</div>

<div>
  Clinically, this is last line compared to other drugs (even
  though more effective / less side fx) because it costs $$$
  compared to the others
</div>
