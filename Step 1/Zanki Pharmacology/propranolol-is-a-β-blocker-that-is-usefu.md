  <b>Propranolol</b> is a <u>β-blocker</u> that is useful for the
  treatment of <b>{{c1::thyroid}} storm</b>

### Extra
<div>
  <i>- blocks increased sympathetic activity as well as inhibiting
  5'-deiodinase</i>
</div>
<div>
  <i>- also may useful for <u>symptomatic</u> relief of
  hyperthyroidism e.g. hypertension, tachycardia, heat intolerance;
  <b>does not help with exophthalmos or myxedema</b></i>
