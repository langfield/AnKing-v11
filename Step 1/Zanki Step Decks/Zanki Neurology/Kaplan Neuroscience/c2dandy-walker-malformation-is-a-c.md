  <b>{{c2::Dandy-Walker}} malformation</b> is a <u>congenital
  failure</u> of the <b>{{c1::cerebellar vermis}}</b> to develop
</div>

### Extra
- Disruption of <b>HOX</b> gene expression in
<u>rhombencephalon</u> disrupts mesenchymal development in the
<u>metencephalon</u>
<div>
  - this disrupts angiogenesis, causing choroid plexus to undergo
  <u>excessive development</u>
</div>
<div>
  - overgrown choroid plexus <u>disrupts microglia</u> with
  phagocytic activity required for <u>midline formation</u> of the
  vermis --> agenesis of the vermis
</div>
