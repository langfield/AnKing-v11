### Extra
<i>thus causing:</i>
<div>
  <i>- ataxia (cerebellum damage / spinocerebellar tracts
  damage)</i>
</div>
<div>
  <i>- loss of vibratory sense/proprioception (dorsal column
  damage)</i>
</div>
<div>
  <i>- muscle weakness and loss of DTRs (lateral corticospinal
  tract and dorsal root ganglia damage)</i>
