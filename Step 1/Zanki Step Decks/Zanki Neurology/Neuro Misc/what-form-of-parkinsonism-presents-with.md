### Text
What form of parkinsonism presents with <u>rapidly</u> progressive
gait dysfunction, falls, executive function loss, and <b>vertical
gaze palsy</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::Progressive supranuclear palsy}}
</div>

### Extra
- Results from neurodegeneration of the midbrain and frontal
subcortical white matter; brain biopsy usually shows deposits of
abnormally phosphorylated tau proteins
<div>
  - imaging hallmark is the "hummingbird / penguin sign" which
  describes the appearance of the brainstem
</div>
