### Text
The <b>infraorbital nerve</b> is a continuation of the
<b>{{c2::maxillary}} nerve</b> that runs across the {{c1::orbital}}
floor

### Extra
<div>
  - the maxillary nerve traverses the foramen rotundum to reach the
  pterygopalatine fossa, then courses through inferior orbital
  fissure and becomes the infraorbital nerve
</div>- infraorbital nerve then travels in a groove in the maxilla
before exiting the skull just inferior to the orbit
