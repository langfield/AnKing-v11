### Text
The symptoms of <b>lumbar</b> <b>spinal stenosis</b> are
<u>exacerbated</u> when the lumbar spine is {{c1::extended}}, and
<u>relieved</u> when the lumbar spine is {{c1::flexed}}

### Extra
This is known as being <b>posture-dependent</b>; thus standing /
walking upright worsens symptoms, walking uphill / leaning on a
stroller or shopping cart makes them better
