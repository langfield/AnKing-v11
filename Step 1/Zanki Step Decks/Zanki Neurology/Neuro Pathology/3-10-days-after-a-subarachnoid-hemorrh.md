### Text
<div>
  <b>3 - 10 days</b> after a <u>subarachnoid hemorrhage</u>,
  {{c1::vasospasm}} can occur in the areas surrounding the ruptured
  aneurysm, which may cause an <b>ischemic</b> <b>infarct</b>
</div>

### Extra
- Major cause of delayed morbidity and mortality (occurs in in
approximately 20 - 30 % of patients); vasospasm occurs due to
impaired brain autoregulation
<div>
  - CT may fail to show vasospasm, so transcranial color doppler is
  needed
</div>
