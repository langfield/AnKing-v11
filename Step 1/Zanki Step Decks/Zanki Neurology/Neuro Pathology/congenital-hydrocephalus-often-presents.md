### Text
<b>Congenital hydrocephalus</b> often presents initially with
macrocephaly, muscle {{c1::hyper}}tonicity and
{{c1::hyper}}reflexia, and a(n) {{c2::bulging}} fontanelle

### Extra
- hypertonicity and hyperreflexia result from upper motor neuron
injury caused by stretching of the periventricular pyramidal tracts
<div>
  - untreated hydrocephalus can lead to developmental delay and
  seizures
</div>
