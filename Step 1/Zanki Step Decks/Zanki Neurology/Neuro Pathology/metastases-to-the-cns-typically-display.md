### Text
<b>Metastases</b> to the <u>CNS</u> typically display an area of
central {{c1::necrosis}} with surrounding <i>vasogenic edema</i> at
the <b>grey-white junction on MRI</b>

### Extra
* Glucocorticoids are often prescribed to reduce swelling and palliate symptoms, but the primary tumor site must be identified (usually with chest x-ray and/or CT scans) and biopsied for definitive diagnosis
