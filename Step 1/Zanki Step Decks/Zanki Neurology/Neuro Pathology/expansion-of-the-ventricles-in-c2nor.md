  <b>Expansion of the ventricles</b> in <u>{{c2::normal pressure}}
  hydrocephalus</u> results in <i>distortion</i> of the fibers of
  the <b>{{c1::corona radiata}}</b>
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  This is a white matter sheet that continues ventrally as the
  internal capsule
</div>
<div>
  - carries most of neural traffic from and to cerebral cortex
</div>
<div>
  - distortion of the fibers contributes to <u>urinary
  incontinence</u> as it disrupts the periventricular pathways that
  transmit impulses from the cortex to the sacral micturition
  center
