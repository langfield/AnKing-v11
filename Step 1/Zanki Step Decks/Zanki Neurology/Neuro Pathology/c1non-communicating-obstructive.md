### Text
<div>
  <b>{{c1::Non-communicating (obstructive)}} hydrocephalus</b> is
  caused by <u>structural blockage</u> of CSF within ventricular
  circulation
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  <i>- e.g. cerebral aqueduct stenosis, colloid cyst blocking
  foramen of Monro, tumors, **obstruction of foramina of
  Luschka</i>
</div>
<div>
  - as opposed to communicating hydrocephalus where there is no
  significant blockage of CSF within brain or brainstem
</div>
