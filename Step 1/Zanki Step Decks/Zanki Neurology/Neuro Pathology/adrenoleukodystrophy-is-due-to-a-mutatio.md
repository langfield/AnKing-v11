### Text
<p dir="ltr" style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
<b>Adrenoleukodystrophy</b> is due to a mutation in a(n)
{{c1::peroxisomal}} membrane transporter, preventing <b>very long
chain fatty acids (VLCFAs)</b> from being tagged with
{{c2::<u>coenzyme A</u>}}

### Extra
<div>
  - This is caused by a mutation in ABCD1, a gene coding for ALD, a
  <u>peroxisomal membrane transporter protein</u> which is needed
  for VLCFAs to be transported into peroxisomes where they will be
  tagged with Coenzyme A
</div>
<div>
  <div>

    <b>testes (leydig cells)</b>
