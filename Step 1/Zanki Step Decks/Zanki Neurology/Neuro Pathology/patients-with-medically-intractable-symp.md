### Text
Patients with <i>medically intractable symptoms</i> (ex.
bradykinesia, rigidity) of <b>Parkinsons disease</b> may benefit
from <u>deep brain stimulation (DBS)</u> of the <b>{{c1::Globus
pallidus internus}}</b> or the <b>{{c2::subthalamic nucleus}}</b>

### Extra
This is done with a <u>high frequency</u>DBS which has an
inhibitory effect on the targeted structures; resulting in
disinhibition and increased activity in the thalamus leading to
improved mobility
<div>
  medically intractable means it is not easily controlled by drugs
  (ex. when patients develop On/Off periods)
</div>
