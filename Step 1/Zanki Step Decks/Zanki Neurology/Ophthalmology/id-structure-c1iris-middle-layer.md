    </div>
  </div>
  <div>
    <div>
      <div>
        The iris is composed of two muscles, the sphincter pupillae
        and the dilator pupillae.
      </div>
      <div>
        The sphincter pupillae (inner aspect): - Concentrically
        arranged muscle fibers - Receives parasympathetic
        innervation - Contraction results in pupillary constriction
      </div>
      <div>
        The dilator pupillae (outer aspect): - Radially arranged
        muscle fibers - Receives sympathetic innervation -
        Contraction results in pupillary dilation
      </div>
    </div>
