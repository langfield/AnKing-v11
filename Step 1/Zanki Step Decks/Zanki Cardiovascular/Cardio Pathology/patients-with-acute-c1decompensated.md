### Text
Patients with <b>acute</b> {{c1::<b>decompensated</b>}} <b>heart
failure</b> typically present with progressive {{c3::dyspnea}},
orthopnea, lower extremity edema, along with bilateral crackles /
wheezing (known as {{c2::cardiac asthma}})

### Extra
pulmonary presentation is due to secondary <u>pulmonary edema</u>;
typically precipitated by MI, severe HTN, valvular heart disease,
afib, and drug use
