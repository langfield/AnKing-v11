  Dual antiplatelet therapy with <u>aspirin</u> and a <u>P2Y12
  inhibitor</u> lowers <b>morbidity</b> and <b>mortality</b> rates
  in patients with {{c1::NSTEMIs}}

### Extra
<div>
  - Dual antiplatelet therapy should be administered to all
  patients with no contraindications (e.g. allergy, bleeding)
</div>
