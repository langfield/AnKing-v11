### Text
While the presentation of <b>myocarditis</b> is highly variable, it
can include fever, dyspnea, chest pain, and characteristically a
<u>persistent {{c1::tachycardia}} out of proportion to
{{c1::fever}}</u>

### Extra
Normally, fever and tachycardia should run in proportion, in this case the tachycardia is exceptionally high
