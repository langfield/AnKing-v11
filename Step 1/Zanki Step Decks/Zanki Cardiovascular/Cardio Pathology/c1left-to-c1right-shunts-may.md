### Text
{{c1::Left}}-to-{{c1::right}} shunts may cause <b>endocarditis</b>

### Extra
<div>
  * When <b>endocarditis</b> is suspected, an <b>echocardiogram</b>
  is recommended to identify vegetations around the border of the
  valves, within the defect, or on adjacent valves in the direction
  of the high velocity jet created by the shunt
</div>
<div>
  * <b>Endocarditis prophylaxis</b> is not recommonded for
  uncomplicated shunts; <b>surgical repair</b> is recommended for
  severe pulmonary HTN
</div>
