What is the likely <i>diagnosis</i> in a patient with <b>peripheral
edema</b>, <b>ascites</b>, <b>JVD <i>without</i> inspiratory
decline</b>, and <b>pericardial calcifications</b> on CXR?

  {{c1::Constrictive pericarditis}}
</div>

### Extra
