### Text
<div>
  <b>Mitral valve prolapse</b> is caused by {{c1::myxoid}}
  <b>degeneration</b> of the valve, making it floppy
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  <i>- in <b>Marfan syndrome</b> this is caused by TGF-B activation
  of Fibroblasts on the mitral valve, resulting in myxedema;
  <b>cardiac failure secondary to this is #2 cause of death in
  marfans patients</b></i>
</div>
<div>
  <i>- <u>Classic type</u> of <b>Ehlers-Danlos</b></i>
  <i><b>syndrome</b> leads to defective type V collagen, leading to
  faulty structure of the mitral valve</i>
</div>
