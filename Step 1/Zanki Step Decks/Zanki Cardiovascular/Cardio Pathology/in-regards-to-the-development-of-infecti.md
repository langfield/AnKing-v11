### Text
In regards to the development of <b>infective endocarditis;</b>
{{c1::mitral valve prolapse}} is the most common underlying
valvular disease in <u>developed countries</u>, whereas
{{c1::rheumatic heart disease}} is the most common underlying
valvular disease in <u>non-developed countries</u>

### Extra
- mitral valve prolapse is even more likely when it is associated
with coexistant <b>mitral regurgitation</b>, platelets and fibrin
deposit which are then colonized by organisms
<div>
  - strep viridans especially loves mitral valve prolapse'd valves
  (dextrans binds to fibrin)
</div>
