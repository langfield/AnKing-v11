### Text
Patients with <b>severe aortic stenosis</b> depend upon
{{c1::atrial contraction}} for maintenance of <u>preload</u>

### Extra
Because aortic stenosis results in concentric LV hypertrophy (and
thus reduced LV compliance), they become dependent on atrial
contraction to maintain adequate LV filling
<div>
  without atrial contraction (ex <b>atrial fibrillation</b>), LV
  preload can decrease to the point of causing in these patients
  <u>severe hypotension</u> and <u>acute pulmonary edema</u>
</div>
