### Text
Patients with <b>{{c1::carotid sinus}} hypersensitivity</b> are at
increased risk of <u>syncope / lightheadedness</u> when pressure is
applied around the neck (ex. a tight shirt collar)

### Extra
These patients carotid sinuses are hypersensitive to pressure, and this will transmit a reflex via the hering branch of the glossopharyngeal nerve to the medullary solitary nucleus, which will then increase vagal tone to the heart
