### Text
<div>
  <b>Buerger disease</b> (thromboangiitis obliterans) is a form of
  segmenting {{c1::medium}}-vessel vasculitis that extends into
  contiguous {{c2::veins}} and {{c2::nerves}}
</div>

### Extra
extension into veins and nerves is rarely seen in other types of
vasculitis; commonly involves the tibial and radial arteries
<div>
  this relatively spares the vessel wall (intact internal elastic
  lamina)
