### Text
<div>
  What effect does the <b>passive leg raise</b> maneuver have on
  the <u>intensity</u> of <b>hypertrophic cardiomyopathy
  murmurs</b>?
</div>
<div>
  {{c1::Decreased intensity}}
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  * passive leg raise causes gravity to pull blood from the legs,
  thus increasing venous return (preload);
</div>
<div>
  * causes LV to stretch and decreases obstructive flow of a
  hypertrophied heart, thus the murmur intensity decreases
</div>
