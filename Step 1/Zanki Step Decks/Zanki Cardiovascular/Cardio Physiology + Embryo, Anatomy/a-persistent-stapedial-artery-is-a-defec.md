### Text
A <b>persistent stapedial artery</b> is a defect of the {{c1::2nd}}
aortic arch that presents with a <u>pulsatile tinnitus</u> and
<u>hearing loss</u>

### Extra
Hearing loss is both conductive (ankylosing of stapes) and sensorineural (erosion of otic capsule)
