### Text
<div>
  What equation is used to calculate <b>mean arterial pressure</b>
  using a patient's <u>cardiac output</u>?
</div>
<div>
  {{c1::MAP = CO x TPR}}
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  <i>MAP = mean arterial pressure (mmHg)</i>
</div>
<div>
  <i>CO = cardiac output (mL/min)</i>
</div>
<div>
  <i>TPR = total peripheral resistance (mmHg/mL/min)</i> or SVR
  (systemic vascular resistance)
</div>
