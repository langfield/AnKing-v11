### Text
<div>
  The <b>Cushing reaction</b> is a <u>triad</u> of
  {{c1::hypertension}}, {{c2::bradycardia}}, and
  {{c3::respiratory}} depression due to <u>increased</u>
  <b>{{c4::intracranial}} pressure</b>
</div>

### Extra
<i>increased ICP constricts arterioles --> ischemia -->
decreased pH and increased CO<sub>2</sub> --> compensatory
peripheral vasoconstriction (hypertension) --> bradycardia
(baroreceptor reflex)</i>
<div>
  <i>symptoms are the OPPOSITE of shock</i>
</div>
