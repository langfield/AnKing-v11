</div>

### Extra
Thus the duration of diastole is the major limiting factor for
coronary blood supply to myocardium; increased HR will then make
coronary blood flow peak even earlier (as it shortens time of
ventricular relaxation)
<div>
  this does not mean there is no blood flow during other phases, it
  just peaks during this time
</div>
