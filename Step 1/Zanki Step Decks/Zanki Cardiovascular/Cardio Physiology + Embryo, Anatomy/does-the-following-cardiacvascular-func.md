### Extra
Chronic anemia causes:
<div>
  - increase in CO to meet metabolic demands of tissue (increased
  inotropy --> cardiac function curve shifts upwards)
</div>
<div>
  - slight increase in venous return due to decreased blood
  viscosity (resulting in a slight clockwise rotation of vascular
  function curve)
</div>
