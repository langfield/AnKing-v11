### Extra
<div>
  - This needs to get blood from epicardial vessels found on the
  most outer layer of the heart, thus the blood supply is furthest
  away (and is therefore the subendocardium is the most susceptible
  to ischemia)
</div>
<div>
  - additionally, the systolic reduction in coronary blood flow is
  greatest in the subendocardium, thus the left ventricle
  subendocardium is especially predisposed to ischemia / infarc
</div>
