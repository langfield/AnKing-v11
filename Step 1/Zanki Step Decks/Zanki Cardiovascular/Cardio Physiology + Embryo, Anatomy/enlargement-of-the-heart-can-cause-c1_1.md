### Text
<div>
  Enlargement of the <u>heart</u> can cause {{c1::hoarseness}} due
  to <b>compression of the left recurrent laryngeal nerve</b>
</div>

### Extra
- this is known as "Ortners's Syndrome" - cardiovascular disease
causing a recurrent laryngeal nerve palsy
<div>
  - this nerve supplies all intrinsic muscles of larynx except
  cricothyroid, thus a unilateral injury of this nerve will cause
  reduced mobility of one of the vocal folds
</div>
<div>
  - patients will also have minor dyspnea and aspiration problems
  (especially liquid)
</div>
