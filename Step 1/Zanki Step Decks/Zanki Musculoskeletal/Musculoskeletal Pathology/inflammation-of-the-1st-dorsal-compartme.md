### Text
Inflammation of the 1st dorsal compartment in <b>De Quervain
Tenosynovitis</b> is due to <u>thickening / swelling</u> of the
{{c1::Extensor Retinaculum}}, which causes friction with tendon
movement

### Extra
- The <b>Extensor Retinaculum (aka Dorsal carpal ligament)</b> is a
thickened part of antebrachial fascia that holds the tendons of the
extensor muscles in place; its friction against the tenosynovial
sheath results in inflammation
