### Text
<b>Limb Compartment Syndrome</b> presents with <u>severe
{{c1::pain}}</u> and <u>tense, swollen compartments</u> that
worsens with <b>limb</b> {{c1::<b>flexion</b>}}

### Extra
- 6P's = Pallor, Pain out of proportion, Paresthesias,
Pulselessness, Poikilothermia (can't regulate body temp), Paralysis
(late finding)
<div>
  - can present like an arterial thrombosis (arterial
  insufficiency) without the massive swelling; but arterial
  thrombosis can lead to compartment upon reperfusion
</div>
