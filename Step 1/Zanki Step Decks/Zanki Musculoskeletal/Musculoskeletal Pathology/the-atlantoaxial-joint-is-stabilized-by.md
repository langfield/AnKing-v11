{{c1::<b>transverse</b>}} ligament, <b>apical</b> ligament, and
<b>alar</b> ligaments

<div>
  - the transverse ligament <b>anchors</b> the dens in place; thus
  an injury of the transverse ligament may lead to <u>atlantoaxial
  instability</u>
</div>
