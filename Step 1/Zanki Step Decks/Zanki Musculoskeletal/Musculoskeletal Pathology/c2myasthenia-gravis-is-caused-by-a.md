### Text
<div>
  <b>{{c2::Myasthenia gravis}}</b> is caused by
  <u>autoantibodies</u> to <b>{{c1::post}}-synaptic</b>
  <b>{{c1::ACh receptors}}</b> at the NMJ
</div>

### Extra
- Importantly, these antibodies (IgM and/or IgG) <b>block</b>
activation resulting in a reduced number of functional
acetylcholine receptors at the neuro muscular junction
<div>
  - This results in a <b>decreased</b> end plate potential (not
  allowing muscle cells to depolarize) since there are less
  postsynaptic cation channels that can now open in response to
  acetylcholine

</div>
