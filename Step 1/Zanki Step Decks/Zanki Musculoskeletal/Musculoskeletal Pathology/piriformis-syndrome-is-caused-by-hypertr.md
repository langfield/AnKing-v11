### Text
<b>Piriformis syndrome</b> is caused by hypertrophy / muscle injury
to the piriformis compressing structures traveling through the
{{c1::greater sciatic foramen}}

### Extra
- <b>Sciatic nerve</b> (which is impinged), internal pudendal
vessels, and and inferior gluteal vessels cross below the
piriformis
<div>
  - structures that cross above the piriformis include superior
  gluteal nerve and gluteal vessels
</div>
