### Text
<b>Osgood-Schlatter Disease</b> is an <u>overuse injury</u> caused
by inflammation of the apophysis located on the {{c1::<b>Tibial
Tuberosity</b>}}

### Extra
<div>
  - these patients present with <b>progressive anterior knee
  pain</b>
</div>- repeated stress on this point results in <b>microtrauma</b>
and <b>microavulsion</b> (proximal patellar tendon insertion
separating from the tibial tubercle)
