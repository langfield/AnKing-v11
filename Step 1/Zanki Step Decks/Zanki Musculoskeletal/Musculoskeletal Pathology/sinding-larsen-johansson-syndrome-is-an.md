### Text
<b>Sinding-Larsen-Johansson Syndrome</b> is an <u>overuse
injury</u> caused by inflammation of the apophysis located on the
{{c1::<b>inferior pole of the patella</b>}}

### Extra
<div>
  very similar to <b style="">Osgood-Schlatter disease</b>
</div>
<div>
  repeated stress on tendon during a time of rapid bone growth (Ex
  puberty) can result in inflammation on the apophysis
