### Text
In muscle contraction, after the muscle cell is depolarized, the action potential travels down the {{c1::T-tubule}} and to the {{c2::dihydropyridine receptor::long name}}

### Extra
The T-tubule is important for allowing depolarization to rapidly
propagate through the interior of the muscle fiber, ensuring
synchronized calcium release and contraction of myofibrils
