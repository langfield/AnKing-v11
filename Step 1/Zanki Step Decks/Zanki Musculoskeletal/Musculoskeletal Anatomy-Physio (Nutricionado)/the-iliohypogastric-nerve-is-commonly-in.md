### Text
The iliohypogastric nerve is commonly injured due to <b>post
abdominal surgery {{c1::sutures}}</b>

### Extra
Common injured in laparotomy, laparoscopic surgery, hernia surgery,
hysterectomy, appendectomy
<div>
  - in these surgeries, surgeons commonly make <b>transverse
  incisions</b> across lower abdomen or pelvis
</div>
<div>
  - when the surgery is done and wound sutured, sutures may trap
  this nerve (especially at edge)
</div>
