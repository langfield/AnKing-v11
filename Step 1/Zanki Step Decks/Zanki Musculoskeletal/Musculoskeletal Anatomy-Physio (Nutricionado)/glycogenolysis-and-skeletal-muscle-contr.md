### Text
<b>Glycogenolysis</b> and <b>skeletal muscle contraction</b> are
synchronized via intracellular accumulation of {{c1::Ca2+}}
following neuromuscular stimulation

### Extra
- Ca2+ (along with cAMP) stimulate phosphorylase kinase, which
activates glycogen phosphorylase
<div>
  - this synchronization provides the energy necessary for
  anaerobic muscle contraction
</div>
