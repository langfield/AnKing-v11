### Text
The obturator nerve is commonly injured due to <b>surgery</b> of
the {{c1::<b>posterior</b>}} <b>pelvis</b> using a <u>trocar</u>

### Extra
<div>
  - To access the obturator nerve you must go in the pelvis (thus
  more commonly injured in pelvic surgery) whereas the femoral
  nerve runs through the pelvis (thus more commonly injured in
  pelvic fracture)
</div>
<div>
  - other causes include pelvic trauma, tumors (ex. <b>bladder
  cancer</b>)
</div>
