### Text
Patients with an <b>intervertebral disc herniation</b> affecting
the {{c2::L5}} or {{c1::S1}} nerve roots will have a positive
{{c1::<u>straight leg raise</u>}} test

### Extra
- Raising a straight leg at 30-70* of hip flexion will elicit pain
and paresthesia in the leg
<div>
  - the "bowstring sign" is when this test is aggravated by
  compression of the popliteal fossa
</div>
