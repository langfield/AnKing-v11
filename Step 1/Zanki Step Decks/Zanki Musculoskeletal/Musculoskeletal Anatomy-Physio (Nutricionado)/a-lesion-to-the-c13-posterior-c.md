### Text
A lesion to the #{{c1::3 - posterior}} cord would result in
{{c2::wrist drop}}, paralysis of the {{c3::deltoid}}, and
impairment of {{c4::internal}} rotation of the humerus

### Extra
<i>Wrist drop can also be seen in #<b>6: radial nerve injury</b>,
deltoid paralysis can also be seen in <b>#5 axillary nerve
injury</b> - this is because posterior cord gives off both</i>

  <i>IF lesion occurs at the bifurcation of the posterior cord,
