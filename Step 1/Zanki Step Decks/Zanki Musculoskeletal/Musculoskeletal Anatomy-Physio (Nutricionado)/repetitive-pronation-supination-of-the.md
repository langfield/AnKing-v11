### Text
<b>Repetitive pronation / supination of the forearm</b> can result
in a(n) {{c1::deep radial}} nerve palsy due to irritation by the
<u>superficial</u> and <u>deep</u> parts of the {{c2::supinator}}
muscle

### Extra
- this portion of the deep radial nerve runs through the supinator
canal (created by the superficial and deep parts of the supinator)
<div>
  - injury in this location can also occur due to direct trauma or
  <u>dislocation of the radius</u>
</div>
