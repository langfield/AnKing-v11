<div>
  - the {{c1::H}} and {{c1::I}} bands, and distance between Z
  bands, are shortened
</div>
<div>
  - the {{c2::A}} band remains the same length
</div>

### Extra

filaments</b> and straddles the M line
<div>
  - A band corresponds to the thick filaments and includes portions
  overlapped by thin filaments
