### Text
Palsy of the deep radial nerve as it travels through the
<u>supinator canal</u> most commonly manifests with "{{c1::finger}}
drop"

### Extra
- weakness on <b>finger / thumb extension</b>
<div>
  - triceps brachii (elbow extension) is fine (nerves are given off
  before the spiral groove)
</div>
<div>
  - extensor carpi radialis longus (wrist extension) is fine
  (nerves are given off between the spiral groove and cubital
  fossa)
</div>
<div>
  - cutaneous sensory to radial distribution is spared
</div>
