### Text
<div>
  Living at <b>high altitude</b> chronically causes <u>hypoxia</u>
  which <u>increases</u> the synthesis of
  {{c1::<b>erythropoietin</b>}}, causing <b>polycythemia</b>
</div>

### Extra
<i>Chronic hypoxia is detected by HIF1a, which stimulates
interstitial fibroblasts of the renal peritubular capillaries to
secrete EPO</i>EPO increases hematocrit and Hb which increases
O<sub>2</sub> transport, but <u>increases</u> blood viscosity
