### Text
To <u>anteriorly</u> aspirate fluid from a patient's pleural
cavities without hitting any other organs (spleen, liver, lung),
ideally a needle should be injected into the {{c1::7th::number}}
intercostal space

### Extra
<div>

  ICS, as the ribs cover the liver (and can be from the 6th to the
  10th ICS)
