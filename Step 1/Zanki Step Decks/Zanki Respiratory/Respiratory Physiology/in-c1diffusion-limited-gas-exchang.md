### Text
<div>
  In <b>{{c1::diffusion}}-limited gas exchange</b>, the gas <u>does
  not equilibrate</u> by the time blood reaches the end of the
  capillary
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  <div>
    For CO, the alveolar membrane is a major barrier for diffusion
    and it takes a bit for molecules to make their way through
    membrane; thus it will take longer for partial pressure of CO
    to rise in blood
  </div>
  <div>
    - This means even when you perfuse more blood past the membrane
    than normal, you WON'T pick up more blood, CO will not have
    enough time to equalize with alveolar pressure when passing
    through capillaries
  </div>
</div>
