### Extra
<div>
  - Corticosteroids accellerate <b>maturation</b> of <u>type II
  pneumocytes</u>
</div>- Thus in patients at risk for preterm labor, it is important
to use tocolytics to delay delivery and then administer
corticosteroids to increase surfactant production
