### Text
<b>Cystic fibrosis</b> may present with <b>{{c1::hypo}}natremia</b>

### Extra
- Because CFTR is not there to resorb chloride at sweat ducts, Na+
gets trapped on the sweat in the skin, leading to excessive <u>salt
wasting</u>
<div>
  - risk factors include exclusive breast or formula feeding prior
  to introduction of Na-rich solid foods, exposure to
  high-temperature environments, and exercise
</div>
<div>
  <div>
    - thus, <u>salt supplementation</u> is recommended for patients
    with CF
  </div>
</div>
