### Text
Patients with <u>long standing</u> <b>COPD</b> who are administered
<u>excessive</u> oxygen can develop <b>{{c1::hypercapnia}}</b>

### Extra
this is due to a reversal of hypoxia induced pulmonary
vasoconstriction that was compensating for poorly ventilated areas,
resulting in increased blood flow to areas that have poor
ventilation
