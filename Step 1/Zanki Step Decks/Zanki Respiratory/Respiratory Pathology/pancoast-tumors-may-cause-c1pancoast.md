<div>
  <b>Pancoast tumors</b> may cause {{c1::<u>Pancoast</u>}}
  <u>syndrome</u>, which is characterized by <b>shoulder pain</b>,
  <b>Horner syndrome</b>, <b>atrophy of hand/arm muscles</b>, and
  <b>edema</b> (due to vascular compression)
