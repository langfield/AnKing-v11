### Text
Endothelial dysfunction of the <b>pulmonary vasculature</b> results
in increased {{c1::vasoconstrictors}} and decreased
{{c1::vasodilators}}

### Extra
vasoconstrictors = Endothelin; vasodilators = NO, prostacyclins; ex
in left heart disease, pulmonary venous congestion leads to
increase in pulmonary capillary / arterial pressure -->
endothelial dysfunction --> increased <b>vascular tone</b>
