### Text
COPD patients with <b>oxygen induced hypercapnia</b> suffer from
excess oxygen induced generation of increased {{c1::physiologic
dead space}}

### Extra
- hypoxia induced vasoconstriction was originally shunting blood
away from poorly ventilated areas, but overwhelming the lungs with
poor oxygen resets that mechanism --> resulting in poorly
ventilated spaces receiving blood flow and generating physiologic
dead space
