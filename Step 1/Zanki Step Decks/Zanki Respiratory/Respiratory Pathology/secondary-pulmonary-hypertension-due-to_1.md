### Extra
<u>High Flow</u> states can lead to pulmonary HTN (P<sub>pulm
artery</sub> = <b>CO</b> x PVR + PL atrium)
<div>
  - High Flow states are caused by large left to right shunts
  (ASD/VSD/PDA)
</div>
<div>
  <u>High LA Pressure</u> states can lead to pulmonary HTN
  (P<sub>pulm artery</sub> = CO x PVR + <b>P<sub>L
  atrium</sub></b>)
</div>
<div>
  - high LA pressure is caused by systolic dysfunction, valvular
  disease (Mitral Lung)
</div>
