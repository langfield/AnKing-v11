What is the <b>venous drainage</b> of the <b><u>left</u>
ovary/testis</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::Left gonadal vein -> <u>left renal vein</u> -> IVC}}

varicocele
<div>
  - left sided renal cell carcinoma
</div>

  crosses over the aorta to the IVC)
</div>
<div>
