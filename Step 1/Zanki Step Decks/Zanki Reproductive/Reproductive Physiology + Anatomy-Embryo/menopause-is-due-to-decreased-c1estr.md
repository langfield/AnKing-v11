### Text
<b>Menopause</b> is due to <u>decreased</u> {{c1::estrogen}}
production due to age-linked decline in the number of ovarian
follicles

### Extra
Additionally, estrogen deficiency leads to decreased glycogen
production in the vaginal epithelium
<div>
  - this results in flattening of labial folds / vaginal rugae
  --> dyspareunia
</div>
