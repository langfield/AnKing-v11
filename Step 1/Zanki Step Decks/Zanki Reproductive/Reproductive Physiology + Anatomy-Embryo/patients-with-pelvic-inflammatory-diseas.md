### Text
Patients with <b>pelvic inflammatory disease</b> or a
<u>ruptured</u> <b>ectopic pregnancy</b> may have
<b>{{c1::culdocentesis}}</b> performed to extract and ascertain the
fluid contents

### Extra
Culdocentesis is using a needle to extract fluid from the <u>pouch
of douglas</u> (<b>posterior</b> <b>cul-de-sac</b>) by inserting a
needle through the posterior fornix of the vagina
