### Text
The <u>nonsurgical treatment</u> for <b>endometriosis</b> consists
of {{c1::NSAIDs}} and {{c1::OCPs}}

### Extra
<div>
  <i>* <b>NSAIDs</b> for to reduce prostaglandins for pelvic pain
  and <b>OCPs</b> cause (-) feedback to reduce estrogen stimulation
  of endometrial tissue.</i>
</div>
<div>
  <i>* Laparoscopy is reserved for <b>treatment failure</b>,
  <b>adnexal mass</b>, or <b>infertility</b></i>
</div>
