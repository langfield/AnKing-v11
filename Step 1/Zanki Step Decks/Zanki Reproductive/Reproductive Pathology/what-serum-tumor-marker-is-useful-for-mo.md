### Extra
<div>
  <i>- this protein is produced by ovarian epithelia and is usually
  markedly elevated in cancerous ovarian cells compared to normal
  cells</i>
</div>
<div>
  <i>- thus not good for screening, as other conditions that
  disrupt ovarian epithelia (ex endometriosis) will cause increased
  release of CA-125 into the blood stream</i>
