Are the following <u>protective</u> factors or <u>risk</u> factors
for surface epithelial ovarian cancer?
<div>
  {{c1::Risk factors}}
  <div>
    <font color="#AA0000">- Infertility</font>
  </div>
  <div>

  transformation
