### Text
<div>
  Patients with <b>polycystic ovarian syndrome</b> have increased
  risk of <b>{{c1::endometrial}} carcinoma</b>, secondary to
  <u>unopposed</u> effects of estrogen
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  <i>- excess androgen leads to anovulatory cycles (prevents
  dominant follicle formation), leading to <u>decreased
  progesterone</u> secretion; additionally estrone production from
  adipose tissue</i>
</div><i>- imbalance leads to long term risk for endometrial
hyperplasia and adenocarcinoma</i>
