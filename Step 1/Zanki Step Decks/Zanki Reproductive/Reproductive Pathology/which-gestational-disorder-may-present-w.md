### Text
Which <u>gestational disorder</u> may present with <i>minimal
vaginal bleeding</i>, <b>focal</b> <b>pain</b>, and a <b>distended
uterus</b> with <b>high-frequency contractions</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::Concealed placental abruption}}
</div>

### Extra
* <b>Focal pain</b> (e.g. back pain, suggesting posterior placental
rupture), an <b>abnormally distended uterus</b> (e.g. measuring 38
cm at 35 weeks gestation), and <b>high-frequency contractions</b>
due to increasing intrauterine pressure and volume
