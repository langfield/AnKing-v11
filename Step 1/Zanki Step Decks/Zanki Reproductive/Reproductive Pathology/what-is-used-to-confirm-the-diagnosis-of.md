### Text
What is used to <u>confirm</u> the diagnosis of a suspected
<b>struma ovarii</b> (subtype of cystic teratoma) after <i>pelvic
ultrasound</i>?
<div>
  {{c1::Pelvic radioimaging (shows uptake of iodine-123 or
  iodine-131)}}
</div>

### Extra
* If struma ovarii is suspected, <b>pelvic ultrasound</b> should be
obtained, and if pelvic ultrasound reveals an ovarian mass, pelvic
radioimaging (using iodine-123 [123-I] or iodine-131 [131-I]) is
performed to confirm the presence of functional thyroid tissue
within the mass
