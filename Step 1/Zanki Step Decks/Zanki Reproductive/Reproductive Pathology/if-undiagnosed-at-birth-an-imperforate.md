### Text
If undiagnosed at birth, an <b>imperforate hymen</b> presents with
{{c1::<u>primary amenorrhea</u>}} and <u>cyclic abdominal / pelvic
pain</u> due to accumulation of {{c2::<b>menstrual bloo</b>d}} in
the vagina / uterus

### Extra
This accumulation is known as <b>hematocolpos</b>, the pressure
build up may cause back pain and difficulties with defecation
