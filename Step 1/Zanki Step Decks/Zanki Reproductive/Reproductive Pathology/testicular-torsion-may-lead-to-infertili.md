### Text
Testicular torsion may lead to <u>infertility</u> and thus is
treated with urgent {{c1::surgery}}

### Extra
- surgeries are detorsion (manual or surgical), orchiopexy
(fixation of testicle), or orchiectomy (if nonviable)
<div>
  - <b>MUST</b> treat contralateral testis (anatomic defect is most
  likely bilateral)
</div>
