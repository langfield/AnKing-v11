### Text
If a patient has a <b>varicocele</b>, consider surgical ligation or
{{c1::catheter embolization}} if associated with <u>pain or
infertility</u>

### Extra
- varicocelectomy (isolate dilated/abnormal veins, ligate -
redirecting blood flow to normal veins)
<div>
  - embolization is an IR procedure, catheter is inserted into
  dilated / abnormal veins, then a coil / sclerosant is used to
  clot off veins
</div>
