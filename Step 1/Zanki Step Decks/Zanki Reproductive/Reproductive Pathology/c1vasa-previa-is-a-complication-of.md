### Text
<b>{{c1::Vasa previa}}</b> is a complication of <u>pregnancy</u>
that occurs when <b>fetal vessels</b> run over, or in close
proximity to, the <b>{{c2::cervical os}}</b>

### Extra
<i>frequently associated with <b>velamentous</b> <b>umbilical</b>
<b>cord</b> <b>insertion</b> (cord inserts in chorioamniotic
membrane rather than placenta)</i>
