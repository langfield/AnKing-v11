  What is the <u>most common</u> symptom of <b>leiomyoma</b>
  (fibroids)?
</div>
<div>
  {{c1::Nothing (asymptomatic)>Heavy menstrual bleeding (when
  symptomatic)}}
</div>

### Extra
<i><u>when present</u>, symptoms include <b>abnormal</b>
<b>uterine</b> <b>bleeding</b>, infertility, and pelvic mass</i>
<div>
  <i>- these can be located on the serosal surface of the uterus
  (subserosal), or within the uterine wall (intramural), and below
  the endometrium (submucosal)</i>
</div>
<div>
  <i>- fibroids in the posterior uterus can put pressure on the
  colon, leading to constipation</i>
</div>
