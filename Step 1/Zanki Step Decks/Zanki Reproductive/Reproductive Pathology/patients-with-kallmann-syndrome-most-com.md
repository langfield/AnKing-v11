### Text
Patients with <b>kallmann syndrome</b> most commonly have mutations
in the {{c1::KAL-1}} or FGFR-1 genes

### Extra
KAL-1 encodes for Anosmin-1; In the embryo, GnRH producing neurons
migrate along a trail of anosmin-1 to the hypothalamus from the
olfactory placode, dragging the olfactory nerve fibers along with
it
<div>
  Anosmin-1 works by promoting FGFR8 and FGFR1 complex formation,
  which helps mediate the effects of anosmin-1 on a cellular level
</div>
