  <b>Chronic myeloproliferative disorders</b> (<u>except CML</u>)
  are associated with <b>{{c1::V617F <i>JAK2</i>}} mutations</b>
</div>

### Extra
JAK2 (Janus Kinase 2) is a <b>cytoplasmic tyrosine kinase</b>
located on chromosome 9
<div>
  <div>
    - mutations result in myeloid progenitors developing a
    <u>hypersensitivity to growth stimulating cytokines</u>
  </div>
  <div>
    - this results in unregulated myeloproliferation; cells have
    more growth and longer survival
  </div>
</div>
