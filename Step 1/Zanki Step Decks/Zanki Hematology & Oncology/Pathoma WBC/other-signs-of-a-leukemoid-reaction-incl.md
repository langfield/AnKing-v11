### Text
Other signs of a <b>leukemoid reaction</b> include
{{c1::basophilic::color}} peripherally located "döhle bodies" in
neutrophils, toxic granulation, and cytoplasmic {{c2::vacuoles}}

### Extra
Döhle bodies are ribosomes bound with RER; typically occur in toxic
systemic illness (leukemoid reaction) but can also be seen in burns
/ myelodysplasia
