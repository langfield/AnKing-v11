### Text
<div>
  When exposed to conditions that favor the <u>{{c2::taut}}</u>
  form of Hemoglobin, <b>HbS</b> {{c1::<b>polymerizes</b>}} and
  forms needle-like structures ("sickle cells")
</div>

### Extra
- Ex Deoxygenation, High Altitude, Acidosis, Infection; Dehydration
does as well; these are conditions that favor <b>oxygen
unloading</b>
<div>
  - The taut form exposes a hydrophobic patch, causing HbS
  molecules to aggregate (polymerize) and form long chain polymers
</div>
<div>
  - formation of long chain polymers of HbS precipitates and causes
  sickling
</div>
