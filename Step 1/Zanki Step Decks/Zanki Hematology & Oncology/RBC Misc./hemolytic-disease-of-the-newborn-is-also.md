### Text
<b>Hemolytic disease of the newborn</b> is also known as
{{c1::Erythroblastosis fetalis}}

### Extra
- This occurs when a blood group mismatch (Ex. ABO, Rh, Kell)
between the mother and the fetus results in antibodies crossing the
placental circulation resulting in destruction of fetal RBCs
<div>
  - In terms of incompatibility severity (Rh > Kell > ABO)
</div>
<div>
  - Kell antigen system is polymorphic transmembrane glycoprotein
  that can be used as a blood grouping system; defective in McLeod
  Syndrome (neuroacanthocystosis)
</div>
