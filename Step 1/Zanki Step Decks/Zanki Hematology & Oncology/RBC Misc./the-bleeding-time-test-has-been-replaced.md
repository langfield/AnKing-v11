### Text
The <b>bleeding time</b> test has been replaced by the
{{c1::platelet function analyzer (PFA-100)}}

### Extra
- This monitors the time needed for a patients blood to form a
platelet based hemostatic plug in an in vitro environment
<div>
  - thus abnormal results are seen in disorders where bleeding time
  would be elevated (ex vWF disease, inherited / acquired platelet
  dysfunction, thrombocytopenia)
</div>
