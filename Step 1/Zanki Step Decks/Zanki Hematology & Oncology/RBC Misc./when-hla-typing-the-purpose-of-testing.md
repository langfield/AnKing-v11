### Text
When <b>HLA typing</b>, the purpose of testing for a <u>Mixed
lymphocyte reaction</u> is to test for MHC class {{c1::II (ex
HLA-DR)}}

### Extra
Donor cells are irradiated (which will prevent proliferation); and
then are mixed with non-irradiated recipient cells and thymidine to
see if proliferation occurs
<div>
  <div>
    - if recipient cells share the MHC Class II with donor, donor
    cells do not activate to attack
  </div>
  <div>
    - if recipient cells do NOT share the MHC Class II with donor,
    donor cells activate and proliferate (and incorporate
    radiolabeled thymidine)
  </div>
