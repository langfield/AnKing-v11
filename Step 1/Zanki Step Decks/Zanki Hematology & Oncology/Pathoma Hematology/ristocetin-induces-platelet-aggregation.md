### Text
<div>
  <b>Ristocetin</b> induces <u>platelet aggregation</u> by causing
  {{c1::<b>vWF</b>}} to bind <b>platelet</b> <b>GPIb</b>
</div>

### Extra
<i>lack of vWF or GPIb -> impaired aggregation -> abnormal
test (e.g. von Willebrand disease, Bernard-Soulier disease)</i>
<div>
  <i>- in vWF disease, addition of normal plasma that contains vWF
  will correct platelet aggregation</i>
</div>
<div>
  <i>- in Bernard-Soulier disease, addition of normal plasma that
  contains vWF will not correct platelet aggregation</i>
</div>
