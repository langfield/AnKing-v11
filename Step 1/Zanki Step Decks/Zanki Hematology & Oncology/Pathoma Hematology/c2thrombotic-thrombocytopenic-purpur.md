### Text
<div>
  <b>{{c2::Thrombotic thrombocytopenic purpura (TTP)}}</b> is due
  to <u>inhibition</u> or <u>deficiency</u> of the
  <b>{{c1::ADAMTS13 (vWF metalloprotease)}}</b> enzyme
</div>

### Extra
- vWF is a multimeric protein that exists in a coiled and uncoiled
state (uncoils at areas of high shear stress - ex. endothelial
damage); the uncoiled form is important for platelet binding as
well as normal degradation (via ADAMTS13)
<div>
  - However, ADAMTS13 also helps maintain the size of the vWF
  protein so that it doesn't get too big, <b>ultra large</b> vWF
  proteins can cause diffuse microvascular thrombosis and occlusion
</div>
