calcific & stenotic {{c1::<b>aortic</b>}} <b>valve</b> causes
increased rupture of <u>{{c2::angiodysplasias}}</u>

### Extra
- Angiodysplasia are essentially AVMs of the GI tract; weak small
vessels that are prone to bleeding
