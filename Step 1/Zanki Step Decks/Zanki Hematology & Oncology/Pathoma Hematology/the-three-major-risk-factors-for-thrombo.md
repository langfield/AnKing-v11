  The <i>three</i> major risk factors for <u>thrombosis</u> are
  <b>{{c1::disruption in blood flow (stasis)}}</b>,
  <b>{{c2::hypercoagulability}}</b>, and <b>{{c3::endothelial cell

### Extra

obesity)
<div>
  causes of hypercoagulability include (factor V leiden / protein
  C/S deficiency, OCPs / SERMs / Pregnancy, severe burns, cancer)
</div>

  DVT, glucagonoma)
