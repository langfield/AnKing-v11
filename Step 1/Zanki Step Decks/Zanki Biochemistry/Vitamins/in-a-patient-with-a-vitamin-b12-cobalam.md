### Text
In a patient with a <b>Vitamin B12 (Cobalamin)</b> deficiency,
<b>B9 (Folate)</b> supplementation will mask {{c1::hematological}}
but not {{c1::neurological}} symptoms

### Extra
This will result in a moderate improvement in the hemoglobin level
<div>
  additionally, use of folate alone can worsen demyelination and
  cause abnormal myelin synthesis by depleting the concentration of
  unmethylated cobalamin available for methylmalonyl-CoA processing
  (thus <b>administration alone</b> can <u>precipitate</u> walking
  difficulties and paresthesias)
</div>
