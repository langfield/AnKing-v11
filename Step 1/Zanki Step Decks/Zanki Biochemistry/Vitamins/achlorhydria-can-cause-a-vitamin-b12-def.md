### Text
<div>
  <b>Achlorhydria</b> can cause a <b>vitamin B12 deficiency</b> by
  preventing <u>release</u> of B12 from {{c1::food particles}}
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  Achlorhydria means that production of HCl in gastric secretions
  of stomach / other digestive organs is absent; stomach acid and
  papain help cleave B12 from food particles
</div>
<div>
  achlorhydria can be seen in atrophic gastritis, ménétrier
  disease, <b>chronic PPI use</b>, radiation therapy involving
  stomach, chronic H pylori infection, pernicious anemia,
  Sjögren's, and more acutely in VIPomas
</div>
