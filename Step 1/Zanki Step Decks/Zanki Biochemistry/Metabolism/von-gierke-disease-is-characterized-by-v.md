  increased</u> levels of {{c1::<b>glycogen</b>}} in the liver and

<div>
  Deficiency in Glucose-6-phosphatase results in accumulation of
  Glucose in G6P form
</div>
<div>
  - Liver cannot convert G6P to release it
</div>
<div>
  - This leads to excess incorporation into glycogen / excess
  storage
