The <b>Peroxisome</b> is unique in that it performs
<u>α-oxidation</u> to break down {{c1::Phytanic Acid}}

### Extra
<div>
  - <b>Phytanic acid</b> is a unique branched chain fatty acid that
  is obtained from dairy, ruminant (ex cow) animal fats, and fatty
  fish (ex salmon); broken down into <u>Pristanic acid</u> (which
  can be broken down by several rounds of β-oxidation
</div>- Only organelle capable of performing α-oxidation; Defective
