### Text
<div>
  <div>
    <b>Ribulose-5-phosphate</b> may be converted to
    <b>{{c1::ribose}}-5-phosphate</b> via the enzyme
    <i>phosphopentose isomerase</i> (HMP shunt)
  </div>
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  <i>this reaction also may produce glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate and
  fructose-6-phosphate</i>
</div>
<div>
  This is reversible and thus is governed by the cellular demand
  for ribose 5 phosphate
</div>
<div>
  - if in excess, shunted to make G3P and F6P for ATP generation
</div>
<div>
  - If in demand, enzymes act in reverse to take G3P and F6P to
  make Ribose-5-P for nucleotide synthesis
</div>
