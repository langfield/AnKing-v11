### Text
{{c1::<b>G protein coupled</b>}} <b>receptors</b> bind
glycoproteins and contain three domains; an <u>extracellular
domain</u> for {{c2::ligand}} binding, a <u>transmembrane
domain</u>, and an <u>intracellular domain</u> coupled with
{{c3::heterotrimeric}} G proteins

### Extra
Notably, the G-alpha subunits of these G proteins are associated
with unique pathologies; Gs-alpha with McCune Albright / Albrights
Hereditary Osteodystrophy and Gq-alpha with Sturge-Weber
