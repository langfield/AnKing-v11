### Text
An unaffected individual (with unaffected parents) who has a
<u>sibling</u> affected by an <b>autosomal recessive</b> condition
has a(n) {{c1::2/3}} chance of being a carrier for that condition

### Extra
The individuals chance of then passing on the mutant allele is 1/2
(one allele is affected, one allele is not)
