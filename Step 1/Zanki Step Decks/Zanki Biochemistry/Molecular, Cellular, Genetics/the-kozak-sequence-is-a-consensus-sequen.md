### Text
The <b>Kozak sequence</b> is a consensus sequence in eukaryotes
that {{c1::surrounds}} the <b>start</b> <b>codon</b> and aids
translation initiation

### Extra
The small ribosomal subunit (40S) initially binds to the 5' cap of
mRNA and scans for the <b>AUG</b>; when the AUG is positioned near
the beginning of an mRNA molecule and surrounded by the Kozak
sequence, this serves as the initiator of translation
