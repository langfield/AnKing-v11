### Text
<div>
  <b>{{c1::Cushing}} syndrome</b> is the result of chronic
  <b>excess glucocorticoids</b> by:
</div>
<div>
  1. Adrenal gland hyperplasia
</div>
<div>
  2. {{c2::Pharmacologic doses of glucocorticoids}} (most common)
</div>
<div>
  3. ACTH secreting pituitary adenoma (Cushing <i>disease</i>)
</div>
<div>
  4. Ectopic ACTH production (e.g. small cell lung carcinoma)
</div>
