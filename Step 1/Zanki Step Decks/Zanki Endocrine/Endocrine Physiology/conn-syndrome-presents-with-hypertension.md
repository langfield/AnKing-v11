### Text
<div>
  <b>Conn syndrome</b> presents with <b>hypertension</b>,
  <b>hypokalemia</b>, and <b>metabolic alkalosis</b> due to
  {{c1::increased}} <u>aldosterone</u> levels
</div>

### Extra
<i>also have <b>decreased</b> <b>renin</b> levels; increased ECF
volume causes increased renal perfusion pressure, which inhibits
renin secretion</i>
<div>
  <i>These electrolyte imbalances may cause <b>paresthesias</b> and
  <b>muscle weakness</b> in some patients</i>
</div>
