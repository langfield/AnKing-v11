### Text
<b>{{c2::<i>Central</i>}}</b> <b>diabetes insipidus</b> is caused
by <u>failure</u> of the <i>posterior pituitary</i> to secrete
<b>{{c1::ADH}}</b>

### Extra
- damage to posterior pituitary typically results in <u>transient
central DI</u> as hypothalamic nuclei are intact and axonal
regeneration / hypertrophy can allow adequate ADH release into the
circulation
<div>
  - damage to hypothalamic nuclei results in loss in
  vasopressinergic neurons, which often leads to <u>permanent
  central DI</u>
</div>
