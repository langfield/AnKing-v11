### Text
The <b>adrenal medulla</b> is in the <b>{{c1::inner}}</b>
<b>zone</b> of the adrenal gland and composes approximately
<b>20%</b> of the glandular tissue

### Extra
This is sharply demarcated from the cortex and is composed of
chromaffin cells with a deeply basophilic cytoplasm; activated by
<b>acetylcholine</b>
