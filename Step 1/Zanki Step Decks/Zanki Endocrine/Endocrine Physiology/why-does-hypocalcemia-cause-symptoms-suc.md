### Text
<div>
  Why does <b>hypocalcemia</b> cause symptoms such as
  <b>tingling</b>/<b>numbness</b> and
  <b>twitching</b>/<b>cramping</b>?
</div>
<div>
  {{c1::Decreased <u>extracellular</u> Ca<sup>2+</sup> decreases
  the <u>threshold potential</u> (makes it more negative)}}
</div>

### Extra
<i>thus, it is easier for cells to depolarize to threshold and more
action potentials are fired (threshold potential is closer to
membrane potential)</i>
<div>
  <i>early sign of <b>hypoparathyroidism</b>-especially
  perioral</i>
</div>
