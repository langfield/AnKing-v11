### Text
<b>Hypothyroidism</b> may contribute to development of
{{c1::atherosclerosis}} due to <i>dyslipidemia</i> and
<i>endothelial dysfunction</i>

### Extra
<div>
  * <b>Hypothyroidism</b> causes decreased LDL turnover due to
  decreased expression and activity of LDL receptors <span style=
  "color: rgb(17, 17, 17);">→</span> increased <strong>total
  cholesterol and LDL</strong>.
</div>
<div>
  * It also causes decreased activity of lipoprotein lipase
  <span style="color: rgb(17, 17, 17);">→</span>
  <strong>hypertriglyceridemia</strong>
</div>
