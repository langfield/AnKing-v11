### Text
<div>
  A(n) <b>{{c1::toxic}} multinodular goiter</b> occurs when a
  multinodular goiter has <b>hyperfunctioning follicular cells</b>
  that work <u>independently</u> of <b>TSH</b>, causing increased
  release of T<sub>3</sub> and T<sub>4</sub>
</div>

### Extra
