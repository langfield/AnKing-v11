### Text
<b>Congenital adrenal {{c1::hyperplasia}}</b> occurs due to loss of
one of the four enzymes needed for <u>cortisol synthesis</u>

### Extra
- Most commonly 21-hydroxylase deficiency (90%), most rarely 3B-HSD
deficiency
<div>
  - because there is a loss of cortisol, ACTH is released; and
  whatever enzyme is blocked, precursors will be shunted elsewhere
</div>
