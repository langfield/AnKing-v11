### Text
A person who is taking levothyroxine <b>surreptitiously</b> will
present with an <u>atrophic thyroid</u> and undetectable serum
{{c1::thyroglobulin}}

### Extra
- A small amount of thyroglobulin is released by the thyroid in
normal function, low level suggests noninflammatory
<div>
  - seen in patients who are trying to lose weight / gain energy;
  or in patients with erroneous dosing / OTC supplements with
  animal thyroid tissue
</div>
