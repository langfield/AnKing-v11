### Text
<div>
  <b>{{c2::Tertiary}} adrenal insufficiency</b> is seen in patients
  with chronic exogenous <b>{{c1::steroid}}</b> use, precipitated
  by <b>{{c1::abrupt withdrawal}}</b>
</div>

### Extra
<i>- Chronic exogenous steroids leads to a chronic suppression of
ACTH release, leading to adrenal atrophy over time;</i> <i>thus
relieving steroids abruptly leads to hypoadrenalism</i>
<div>
  <div>
    <i>- therefore chronic adrenal insufficiency patients who go
    for surgery are given a single dose of high dose steroids
    (<u>stress dose steroids</u>) to prevent insufficiency; also
    the basis for weaning people off steroids (<u>slow
    discontinuation</u>)</i>
  </div>
</div>
