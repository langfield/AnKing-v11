### Text
Infection, pain, and sleep deprivation can precipitate {{c1::hyper}}glycemia in patients with insulin treated diabetes

### Extra
These <b>stressful</b> situations increase catecholamine release,
which raises glucose by decreasing pancreatic insulin secretion and
by increasing glycogenolysis and gluconeogenesis
