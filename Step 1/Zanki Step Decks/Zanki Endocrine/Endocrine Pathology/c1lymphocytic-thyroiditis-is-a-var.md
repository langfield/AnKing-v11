### Text
<b>{{c1::Lymphocytic Thyroiditis}}</b> is a variant of hashimoto's
that presents with <u>transient hyperthyroidism</u> followed
sometimes by <u>hypothyroidism</u>

### Extra
- can look like Graves' without eye / skin findings + serum TSI not
present
<div>
  - usually self-limited (weeks)
