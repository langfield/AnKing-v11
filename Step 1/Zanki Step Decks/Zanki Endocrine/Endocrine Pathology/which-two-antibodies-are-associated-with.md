  Which <u>two</u> antibodies are associated with <b>Hashimoto
  thyroiditis</b>?
</div>
<div>
  {{c1::Anti-thyroglobulin}} and {{c2::anti-thyroid peroxidase
  (anti-microsomal)}}
</div>

of damage</i>
<div>
