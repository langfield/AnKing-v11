<b>Pituitary</b> <b>apoplexy</b> may lead to
{{c1::<b>cardiovascular collapse</b>}} due to <b>acute
hypopituitarism</b> causing decreased <u>ACTH</u>

### Extra
* Treatment is <b>rapid volume resuscitation</b> and <b>IV
glucocorticoids</b> (e.g. hydrocortisone, dexamethasone) to
stimulate the adrenal cortex to produce ACTH
