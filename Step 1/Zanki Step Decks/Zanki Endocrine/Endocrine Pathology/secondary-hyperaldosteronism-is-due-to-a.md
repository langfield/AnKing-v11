  <b>Secondary hyperaldosteronism</b> is due to activation of
  <b>{{c1::renin-angiotensin}} system</b>
</div>

### Extra
<i>seen in patients with <b>renovascular hypertension</b> (renal
artery stenosis)<b>,</b> <b>juxtaglomerular cell tumors,</b> and
<b>edema</b> (cirrhosis, heart failure, nephrotic syndrome)</i>
<div>
  <i><u>EDEMA present</u> due to <b>NO</b> <b>Aldosterone Escape
  mechanism</b> (even if ANP rises, ATII is still present
  increasing Na reabsorption through Na/H exchanger)</i>
