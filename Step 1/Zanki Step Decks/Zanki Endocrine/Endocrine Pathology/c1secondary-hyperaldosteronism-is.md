### Extra
<i>seen in patients with <b>renovascular</b> <b>hypertension</b>
and <b>juxtaglomerular cell tumor</b> (due to independent
activation of renin-angiotensin-aldosterone system), as well as
causes of <b>edema</b> (cirrhosis, heart failure, nephrotic
syndrome)</i>
<div>
  <i><u>EDEMA present</u> due to <b>NO</b> <b>Aldosterone Escape
  mechanism</b> (even if ANP is elevated, ATII is still increasing
  Na reabsorption via Na/H exchanger)</i>
</div>
