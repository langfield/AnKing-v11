<b>Hemorrhoids</b> result from distension of the anal
{{c1::arteriovenous plexus}}

### Extra
- this normally forms the anal cushions (composed of venous
sinusoids and CT) which are important for maintaining fecal
continence
<div>
  - chronically increased venous pressure (straining / aging) can
  lead to cushions bulging into the anal canal (can become
  inflamed, thrombose, or prolapse)
</div>
