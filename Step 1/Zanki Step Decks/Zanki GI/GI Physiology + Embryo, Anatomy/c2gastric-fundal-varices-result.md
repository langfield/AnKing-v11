### Text
<b>{{c2::Gastric (fundal) varices}}</b> result from
<u>portosystemic shunting</u> between the <b>{{c1::short gastric
veins}}</b> (portal) and <b>{{c1::inferior phrenic}}</b> (systemic)
<b>vein</b>

### Extra
Aka the <b>Gastrocaval shunt</b>; short gastric veins -->
splenic vein, inferior phrenic --> IVC; appear in <u>portal
hypertension</u> and <u>splenic vein thrombosis</u> (isolated
variceal formation)
<div>
  when ruptured can cause <b>gastric bleeding</b> and <b>melena</b>
