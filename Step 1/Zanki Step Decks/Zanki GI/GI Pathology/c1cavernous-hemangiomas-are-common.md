  <b>{{c1::Cavernous hemangiomas}}</b> are <i>common</i>,
  <u>benign</u> <b>tumors</b> involving the <u>deeper tissues of
  the body</u> (ex. liver, brain) that typically occur from <b>age
  30 - 50 years</b>

### Extra
- these are congenital malformations that enlarge by <b>ectasia</b>
<div>
  <div>
    - may occur singly or in multiples and are well circumscribed,
    present as "mulberry-like" masses of compressible purple
    vascular clusters; grow up to a few centimeters in size
  </div>
  <div>
    - these may appear on skin, mucosa, deep tissues, and other
