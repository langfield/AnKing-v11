### Text
<div>
  {{c1::<b>Rumination</b>}} <b>syndrome</b> is a functional
  gastrointestinal disorder characterized by <u>effortless
  regurgitation</u> of ingested food into the mouth after most
  meals.
</div>

### Extra
There is no retching, nausea, heartburn, odor, or abdominal pain
associated with the regurgitation, as there is with typical
vomiting, and the regurgitated food is undigested.
<div>
  Typically occurs in infants, young children, and patients with
  cognitive disabilities
</div>
