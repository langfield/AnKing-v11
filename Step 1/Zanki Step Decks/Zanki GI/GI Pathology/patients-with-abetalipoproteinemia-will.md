### Text
Patients with <b>Abetalipoproteinemia</b> will have enterocytes
with {{c1::clear}} cytoplasm, prominently at the tips of the villi

### Extra
This is because chylomicrons need ApoB48 to mediate secretion into
Lacteals; without it, chylomicrons build up in the villi epithelium
and fat remains in the intestine
