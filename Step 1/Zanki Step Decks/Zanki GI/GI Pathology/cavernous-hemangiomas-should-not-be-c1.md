### Text
<div>
  <b>Cavernous hemangiomas</b> should <u>NOT</u> be
  {{c1::biopsied}} due to risk of fatal hemorrhage
</div>

### Extra
- These tumors consist of cavernous blood filled vascular spaces of
variable sizes lined by a <u>single epithelial layer</u>, lack of
structural support makes them easily susceptible to damage and
hemorrhage; biopsy also has low diagnostic yield
<div>
  - <b>HOWEVER</b>, if in the brain, surgical resection is
  indicated for lesions that cause intractable epilepsy or
  progressive neurologic deficits
</div>
