### Extra
<div style="font-style: italic;"></div>
<div>
  Ultrasound is the preferred initial imaging, but HIDA scan
  (<b>cholescintigraphy</b>) can be used when ultrasound is
  inconclusive
</div>HIDA scan = hepatobiliary iminodiacetic acid scan;
iminodiacetic acid is radiotracer, <u>failure to visualize</u>
gallbladder suggests <b>obstruction</b> (ex acute / chronic
cholecystitis, biliary atresia)
