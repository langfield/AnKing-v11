### Text
<b>GERD</b> initially presents with <i>dysphagia</i>, but can
progress to {{c1::<i>odynophagia</i>}} due to <u>erosive</u>
<u>esophagitis</u> or <u>esophageal ulcers</u>

### Extra
* odynophagia is <u>painful swallowing</u> whereas dysphagia is
<u>difficulty swallowing</u>
<div>
  * chronic GERD may causes <b>esophageal stricture</b>, as well
</div>
