<div>
  <b>Ménétrier disease</b> is characterized by <u>excess</u>
  <b>mucus production</b> with resultant <b>{{c1::protein}}
  loss,</b> leading to <u>edema</u>
</div>

### Extra
<i>thus also referred to as <b>hypoproteinemic hypertrophic
gastropathy</b></i>
<div>
  <div>
    <i>↑ mucus neutralizes acid of stomach</i>
  </div>
  <div>

    swelling</b> due to hypoalbuminemia)</i>
