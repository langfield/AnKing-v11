<div>
  <b>{{c2::Necrotizing enterocolitis (NEC)}}</b> is characterized
  by necrosis of intestinal mucosa with possible
  <u>perforation</u>, which can lead to:
</div>
<div>
  - <u>free air in the bowel wall</u> ({{c1::<b>pneumatosis</b>
  <b>intestinalis</b>}})
</div>
<div>
  - <u>free air in the abdomen</u>
  ({{c2::<b>pneumoperitoneum</b>}})
</div>
<div>
  - portal venous gas on imaging
</div>
