### Text
Generalizability bias is a form of {{c1::<b>selection bias</b>}}
that is related to the selective referral of patients to
{{c2::<b>tertiary medical centers</b>}}

### Extra
- <i>Tertiary medical centers are academic medical centers</i>
<div>
  <i>This occurs when participants selected for a study do not
  represent the population because they were selected from patients
  at a tertiary medical center</i>
