<b>Migration (attrition) bias</b> is a form of {{c2::<b>selection
bias</b>}} that occurs when patients drop out of the study
{{c1::<b>prematurely</b>}}

### Extra
<div>
  <i>- Only occurs if patients drop out in a <b>non-random
  pattern</b> and characteristics are <b>not on average similar</b>
  between exposure and non exposure</i>
