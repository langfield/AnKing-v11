### Text
Exclusion criteria can result in {{c1::<b>External Validity</b>}}
being compromised if too strict, maximizing {{c2::<b>Internal
Validity</b>}}

### Extra
<i>- Exclusion criteria can make the final study population
becoming so selective that results do not apply to real world
patients</i>
<div>
  <i>- Can be a problem in <b>Generalizability Bias</b></i>
</div>
<div>
  <i><b>external validity=</b>the degree to which inferences from
  the study can be generalized to a broader population</i>
</div>
<div>
  <i><b>internal validity=</b>the degree to which study findings
  lead to correct inferences about results of the study</i>
</div>
