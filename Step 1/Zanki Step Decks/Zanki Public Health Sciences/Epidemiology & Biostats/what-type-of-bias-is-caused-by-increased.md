### Text
What <u>type of bias</u> is caused by <b>increased detection</b> of
diseases (e.g. cancer) with a <b>more</b> <b>benign course</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::Length-time bias}}
</div>

### Extra
- This is seen in tests that detect long latency periods, and thus
potentially missing out on diseases with shorter latency periods
that become symptomatic earlier
<div>
  - ex. a slowly progressive cancer is more likely detected by a
  screening test than a rapidly progressive cancer
</div>
