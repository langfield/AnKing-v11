### Text
In the setting of <b>chronic inflammation</b>, macrophages can be
activated to <b>M2</b> macrophages by stimulation from {{c2::Th2}}
cells, resulting in a(n) <u>{{c1::antiinflammatory}}</u> phenotype

### Extra
This is the <b>alternative</b> activation pathway, and is mediated
by <u>IL-4/IL-13</u>
<div>
  These macrophages are also involved in repair processes
</div>
<div>
  **Tumors typically activate the M2 version and the "repair"
  features helps the tumor survive
</div>
