### Extra
- The non-hereditary form (myositis ossificans traumatica) is due
to trauma to muscle causing bone to form
<div>
  - hereditary (myositis ossificans progressiva) is due to an
  autosomal dominant mutation in the activin type 1 receptor,
  causing endothelial cells to transform to mesenchymal cells and
  then to bone
  <div>
    <i>*note it is separated from the bone-its in the muscle (it is
