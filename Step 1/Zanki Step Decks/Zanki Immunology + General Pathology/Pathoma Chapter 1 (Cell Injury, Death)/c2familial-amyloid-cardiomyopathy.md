<div>
  <b>{{c2::Familial amyloid}} cardiomyopathy</b> /
  <b>polyneuropathies</b> are characterized by deposition of
  <u>mutated</u> {{c1::<b>transthyretin</b> (TTR)}} protein in the
  heart and nerves
</div>

### Extra
<i>- 5% of African Americans carry the mutated gene; mutations in
transthyretin increase the tendency of TTR to misfold (thus
producing an amyloid that infiltrates the myocardium)</i>
<div>
  - these patients will have a low voltage ECG, but a thickened
  heart on echocardiogram
