### Text
Mutations that cause <u>{{c2::overexpression}}</u> of the <b>HER2
receptor</b> can precipitate development of certain <u>Breast* and
Gastric* Cancers</u> by {{c1::<u>amplifying</u>}} the <b>signal
transduction</b>

### Extra
<div>
  - *a subset of breast and gastric cancers that are HER2+; this is
  overexpressed in 20-30% of breast cancers and is associated with
  poorly differentiated, rapidly growing tumors
</div>
<div>
