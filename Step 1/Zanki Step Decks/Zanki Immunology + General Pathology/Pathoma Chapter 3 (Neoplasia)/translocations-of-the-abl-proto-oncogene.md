### Text
Translocations of the <b>Abl</b> proto-oncogene with the
<b>Breakpoint Cluster Region (BCR)</b> on chromosome
{{c1::<u>22</u>}} are associated with:
<div>
  <div>
    - {{c2::Chronic Myelogenous}} Leukemia
  </div>
  <div>
    - {{c2::adult Acute Lymphoblastic}} Leukemia
  </div>
</div>

### Extra
- when seen in adult ALL, this is associated with POOR prognosis
<div>
  <div>
    - the resultant fusion protein (Bcr-Abl) can be treated with
    Imatinib / <b>Dasatinib</b> (specifically designed to inhibit
    this fusion protein)
  </div>
</div>
