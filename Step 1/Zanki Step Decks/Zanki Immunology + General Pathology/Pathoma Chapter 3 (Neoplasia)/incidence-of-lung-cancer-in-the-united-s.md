Incidence of <b>lung cancer</b> in the <u>United States</u> has
decreased in {{c1::men::sex}}, but has only since the 2000's
started to decrease in {{c1::women::sex}}

### Extra

begun to decrease in women (corresponding to decline in tobacco
use)
<div>
  - According to that same statistics report, the differences
  between incidence in men vs women has to do with historical
  patterns in tobacco use (women began smoking in large numbers
  many years later than men)
