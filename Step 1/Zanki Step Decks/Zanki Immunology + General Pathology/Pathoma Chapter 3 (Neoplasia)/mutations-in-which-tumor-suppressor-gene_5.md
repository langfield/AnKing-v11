### Extra
- Merlin is involved in <b>contact inhibition</b>; a behavior
expressed by fibroblast like cells when they adhere to each other
or come into contact
<div>
  - If the cells adhere to each other, this signals each cell to
  stop growing outwards; when cadherins link this leads to
  dephosphorylation of Merlin which causes <u>cell cycle arrest</u>
  and tumor suppression (for as long as the cells are in contact -
  linked by cadherins)
</div>
<div>
  - if mutated, fibroblast like cells divide and grow over each
  other in an uncontrolled manner (<b>important in cancer invasion
  / metastasis</b>)
</div>
