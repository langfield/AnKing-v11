### Text
Epithelial tumors that grow {{c2::<b>exophytically</b>}} are known
as {{c1::<b>papillomas</b>}} when <u>benign</u> and
{{c1::<b>papillary carcinoma</b>}} when <u>malignant</u>

### Extra
- exophytically = finger or warty like projections that are macro
or microscopically visible; these have a core of connective tissue
and a blood vessel
<div>
  - NOTE - this does not refer to growth on an existing papilla
  (nipple), but to the projection created by the tumor
</div>
