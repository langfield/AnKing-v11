### Text
In <b>invasive carcinoma</b>, <u>cell-cell</u> contacts are lost by
inactivation of <b>{{c1::E-cadherin}}</b>

### Extra
<div>
  - Normally, this is how epithelial cells maintain attachment (and
  prevent cellular overgrowth)
</div>
<div>
  - once cells detach via cadherin connections, cells will continue
  to grow outwards and over each other (aided by the resultant
  inactivation of the <u>Merlin</u> tumor suppressor protein)
</div>
