### Text
<div>
  The <b>APC</b> tumor suppresor regulates the <b>Wnt signalling
  pathway</b> by destroying {{c1::<b>B</b>-<b>catenin</b>}} in the
  <u>absence</u> of a Wnt receptor-ligand interaction
</div>

### Extra
- However, in the <u>presence</u> of Wnt-receptor/ligand
interaction, B-catenin is not destroyed and is free to upregulate
proteins involved in <u>cell growth</u> and <u>cell cycle
progression</u>
<div>
  - the Wnt signaling pathway is an important embryological pathway
  involved in <u>body axis patterning</u> and <u>cell fate
  specification</u>, and also plays an important role in <b>adult
  tissue regeneration</b>
</div>
