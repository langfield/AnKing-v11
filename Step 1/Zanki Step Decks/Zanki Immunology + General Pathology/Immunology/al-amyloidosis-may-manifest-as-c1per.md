### Extra
* deposits can lead to <b>waxy, tight skin</b> (epidermal
deposits); <b>macroglossia</b>; and <b>peripheral neuropathy</b>
<div>
  * <b>periorbital purpura</b> may reflect protein infiltration
  causing a bleeding diathesis (e.g. due to factor X sequestration
