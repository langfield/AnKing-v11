  after breakdown of <i>endogenous antigen</i> by the
  {{c1::proteasome}} in the cytosol, and delivery to the RER via
  {{c2::TAP (transporter associated with antigen processing)}}
</div>

  endogenous antigens are tagged by ubiquitin ligases for
  proteasomal degradation, then the cytosolic peptides are taken
