### Extra
<div>
  - When multiple bound IgE on mast cells / basophils cross links a
  multivalent antigen, this pulls the FceRI (high affinity IgE
  receptor) together
</div>
<div>
  - clumping of many FceRI induces signalling that leads to
  degranulation
