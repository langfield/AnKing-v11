### Text
<b>Immunodeficiency</b> (e.g. HIV) <u>increases</u> the risk for
{{c1::<u>non-Hodgkin lymphoma</u>::cancer}}, which presents as
<i>diffuse lymphadenopathy</i> and <i>B symptoms</i>

### Extra
<div>
  * specifically, may see a patient with fever, night sweats, and
  supraclavicular, epitrochlear, mediastinal lymphadenopathy
</div>
<div>
  * <b>diagnosis</b> is usually made by <b>excisional lymph node
  biopsy</b>
</div>* due to immune dysregulation and reactivation of the
Epstein-Barr virus in B-lymphocytes
