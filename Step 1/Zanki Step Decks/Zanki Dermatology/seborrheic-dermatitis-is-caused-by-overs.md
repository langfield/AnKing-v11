### Text
<b>Seborrheic dermatitis</b> is caused by oversecretion of the
{{c1::sebaceous glands}} due to a hypersensitivity reaction to
{{c2::<u>Malassezia</u>}} spp.

### Extra
- Thus seen in areas rich in sebaceous glands (ex. scalp, face,
periocular region); most common cause is <b>malassezia globosa</b>
<div>
  - thus can respond to <u>topical antifungals</u>
</div>
