### Text
The most important steps in working up a <b>metabolic alkalosis</b>
are:
<div>
  <div>
    - determining the patient's {{c1::<b>volume status</b>}} for
    <u>saline responsiveness</u>
  </div>
  <div>
    - checking the <b>urine</b> {{c2::<b>chloride</b>}} levels
  </div>
</div>

### Extra
- vomiting / nasogastric aspiration present with low urine chloride
and volume loss (saline responsive)
<div>
  - prior diuretic use can lead to a low urine chloride state that
  is saline responsive, and current diuretic use can lead to a high
  urine chloride state when diuretic use is ongoing (also saline
  responsive)
</div>
<div>
  - mineralocorticoid excess state will result in a high urine
  chloride and hyper volemia that thus will not be saline
  responsive
</div>
