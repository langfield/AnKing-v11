### Extra
<div>
  <div>
    1 - ureteropelvic junction of the renal pelvis and the ureter -
    pain refers to T10-L1; this starts at the level of the pelvic
    brim
  </div>
  <div>
    <div>
      2 - as the ureter enters the pelvis and crosses over the
      common iliac artery bifurcation - hypogastric; courses
      medially and anteriorly to the bifurcation of common iliac
      arteries
    </div>
    <div>
      3 - the vesicoureteric junction (VUJ) as the ureter obliquely
      pierces the bladder wall (at the intramural part) - S2-S4
    </div>
