### Text
What is the most common cause of urinary tract obstruction in
adults and can cause <u>uni</u><u>lateral</u>
<b>hydronephrosis</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::<b>Urolithiasis</b>}}
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  * <b>Urolithiasis</b> refers to stones originating anywhere in
  the urinary system, including the <u>kidneys</u> <b>and</b>
  <u>bladder</u>
</div>
<div>
  <b>* Nephrolithiasis</b> refers to the presence of such stones in
  the kidneys
</div>
