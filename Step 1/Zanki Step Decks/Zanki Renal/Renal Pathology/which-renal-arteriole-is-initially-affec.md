### Extra
Initially affects the <b>efferent</b> arteriole (causing a
<u>nephrotic</u> syndrome), and then after enough glomerular damage
affects the <b>afferent</b> arteriole (advanced diabetic
nephropathy can present with microscopic hematuria)
