### Text
<u>Decreased</u> dietary intake of what may cause <b>calcium
oxalate kidney stone</b> formation?
<div>
  {{c1::Calcium}} *bonus: why?
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  * Dietary calcium binds oxalate in the gut to form insoluble
  calcium oxalate, which is eliminated in the feces
</div>
<div>
  * This reduces the amount of oxalate absorbed into the body and
  excreted in the urine, reducing stone formation
</div>
