### Text
Patients with <b>renal artery stenosis</b> may eventually develop
renal {{c1::atrophy}}

### Extra
- This is due to ischemia to the kidney (which initially causes the
hypertension), but after time the lack of oxygen and nutrients may
cause atrophy
<div>
  - histologically the atrophic kidney shows crowded glomeruli,
  tubulointerstitial atrophy and fibrosis, and often times focal
  inflammatory infiltrates
</div>
