### Extra
<i>Begins as a nephrotic syndrome, then after ensuing damage can
lead to a nephritic syndrome</i>
<div>
  <i>Arteriolosclerosis of the afferent arteriole results in
  decreased RBF, which leads to infarction and nephritic
  syndrome</i>
