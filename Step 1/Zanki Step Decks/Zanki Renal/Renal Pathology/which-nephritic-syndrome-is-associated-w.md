### Text
Which nephritic syndrome is associated with <u>overactivation</u>
of the <b>alternative</b> <b>complement</b> <b>pathway</b>?
<div>
  {{c1::<b>MPGN II (dense deposit disease)</b>}}
</div>

### Extra
<div>
  * caused by IgG antibodies (termed <em>C3 nephritic factor</em>)
  directed against C3 convertase of the alternative complement
  pathway

  complement activation and kidney damage
