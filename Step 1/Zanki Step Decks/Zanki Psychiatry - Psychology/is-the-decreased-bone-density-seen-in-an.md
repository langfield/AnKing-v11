### Extra
often <b>irreversible</b>, loss of fat reserves results in loss of
leptin production (satiety hormone); leptin indirectly regulates
GnRH neurons and thus loss of leptin --> decreased GnRH
secretion (and thus loss of positive estrogen stimulus on bone
building)
