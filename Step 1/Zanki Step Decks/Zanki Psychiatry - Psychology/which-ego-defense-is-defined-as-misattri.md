  Which <u>ego defense</u> is defined as <b>misattributing</b>
  <u>one's own</u> unconscious, undesired thoughts or <u>feelings
  to another</u> person who does not actually have them?

  {{c1::Projection (immature defense)}}
</div>

### Extra
<i>As opposed to <b>displacement</b>, where you redirect of
emotions or impulses to a neutral object; here projection allows an
individual to be relieved of unnacceptable feelings without the
internal conflict that would occur with self expression</i>e.g. a
man who wants to cheat on his wife accuses his wife of being
unfaithful
