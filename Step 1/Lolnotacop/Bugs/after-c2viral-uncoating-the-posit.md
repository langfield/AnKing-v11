### Text
After {{c2::<u>viral uncoating</u>}}, the <b>positive-sense</b>
genomic <i>RNA</i> of <b>coronavirus</b> can be <u>translated</u>
into {{c1::<i>2</i> long <u>polyproteins</u>}}

### Extra
Can then undergo <b>autoproteolytic cleavage</b> into
<u>proteases</u>. These <u>proteases</u> then cleave the
<i>remainder</i> of the long polyprotein into the other proteins of
<b>coronavirus</b> that will play a large role in translation and
transcription.
