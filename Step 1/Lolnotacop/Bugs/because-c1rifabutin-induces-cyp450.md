### Text
Because <b>{{c1::Rifabutin}}</b> induces CYP450 <i>less</i> than
other drugs in its class (i.e. Rifampin), it is used in the
treatment of <u>{{c2::tuberculosis}}</u> in <b>{{c2::HIV}}</b>
patients

### Extra
<div>
  HIV drugs NRTIs are metabolized by CYP450, therefore the less
  aggressive induction of CYP450 doesn't counteract the dosing of
  NRTIs
</div>
