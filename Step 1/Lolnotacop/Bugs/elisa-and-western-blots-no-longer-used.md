### Text
<b>ELISA and western blots (no longer used)</b> for the
<u>diagnosis of HIV</u> are <i>falsely positive</i> in
<b>infants</b> born to <u>HIV+ mothers</u> since
<b>anti-{{c1::gp120}} antibodies</b> cross the placenta
