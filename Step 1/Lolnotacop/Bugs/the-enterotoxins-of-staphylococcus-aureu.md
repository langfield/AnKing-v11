### Text
The <b>enterotoxins</b> of <i>Staphylococcus aureus</i> are
{{c1::preformed}}, therefore they have a <u>rapid-onset of
action</u>

### Extra
<div>
  Incubation period is very short (2-6 hrs) due to the toxins being
  pre-formed. The enterotoxin is heat stable and hence is not
  destroyed by cooking.
</div>
<div>
  Also seen with Bacillus cereus and reheated rice
</div>
