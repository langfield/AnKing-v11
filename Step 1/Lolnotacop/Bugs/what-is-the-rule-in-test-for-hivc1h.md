FA 2019: Western blot tests are no longer recommended by the CDC
for confirmatory testing. Presumptive diagnosis is made with
HIV-1/2 Ag/Ab immunoassays. These assays detect viral p24 Ag capsid
protein and IgG Abs to HIV-1/2.
