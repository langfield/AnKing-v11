### Text
Once <u>HIV</u> is inside a cell, viral {{c1::reverse
transcriptase}} converts <b>viral RNA</b> to <b>double-stranded
DNA</b> (dsDNA)
<div>
  This <b>double-stranded DNA</b> is <u>inserted</u> into the
  <b>host cell genome</b> by {{c1::integrase}}, another viral
  enzyme
</div>

### Extra
recall that the HIV gene <i>pol</i> encodes reverse transcriptase,
aspartate protease and integrase
